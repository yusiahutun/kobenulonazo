    Lady Gaga's New Lilac Hair While Wearing a Sweatshirt as a Dress

    20 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Lady Gaga loves to surprise us with her hair changes, and has already tried out a plethora of fun hair colors including pastel blue, "ocean blonde," and bubblegum pink. And now, the "Stupid Love" singer has added lilac to that list.

    Posting a photo of her vibrant, new hair color, Lady Gaga wrote on Instagram, "This holiday it’s so important to #bekind #loveyourself #spreadjoynotgerms #staysafe." In the snap, the singer can be seen wearing a pink, off-the-shoulder sweatshirt featuring the slogan, "Be Kind." She's wearing the sweatshirt as a dress, because who needs pants, anyway?

    Unsurprisingly, Gaga's new hair looks totally stunning.

    Just last month, the A Star Is Born actress joined Joe Biden's campaign trail, when she performed in Philadelphia ahead of election day. During the rally, she reminisced about her ex-fiancé, actor Taylor Kinney, and revealed that she used to live with Kinney in his hometown of Lancaster, Pennsylvania.

    Earlier this year, Lady Gaga opened up about the process of writing her latest album, Chromatica, and how making new music had helped her to manage the chronic pain she experiences on account of living with fibromyalgia. She explained to PAPER magazine, "Some days are way worse, some days aren't. But you know what I can do? I can go, 'Well, my hands work; my arms work; my legs work, even though they are sore; my back works; my brain works; my heart works; I'm taking breaths, my lungs work.' You can just be grateful for what you can do."
