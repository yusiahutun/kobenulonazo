Dressing a growing bump is notoriously difficult in the heat, but luckily there are plenty of maternity brands making incredible clothes that fit pregnant ladies and we have rounded up high street looks from M&S, Mango, H&M and more.

    ASOS

    ASOS has possibly one of the largest maternity collections on the high street, and the offering is incredible. Forget elasticated waistbands and baggy t-shirts; here you can shop everything from summer dresses to chic jackets and bikinis. There’s also a great selection from brands, such as Chi Chi London and the North Face.
