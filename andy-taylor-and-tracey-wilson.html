    Andy Met his wife Tracey Wilson while she was the bands hairdresser at her salon Wilson, Wilson and Wilson in Wolverhampton.
    Andy proposed on his 21st Birthday, Tracey was Ill in bed, Andy held her hand and asked her to marry him, she never said anything, so Andy thought she hadn't heard him, until Tracey awoke at 4 am to say yes, Andy said "yes, what?" then he remembered!
    There wedding was postponed several times due to work commitments, they finally tied the knot in Hollywood, California in July 1982 whilst on tour, they asked the dean of a nearby university to perform the service, They rented the Chateau Marmont and the group were formally dressed in Top hats, Morning suits, pearl tie pins, and pin stripe trews for the service John performed the duty of best man.
    Andy said:
    " John was shaking like a leaf!"
    Tracey flew in from her home in Wolverhampton, unfortunately her parents were unable to join them.
