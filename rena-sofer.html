    Early Life of Rena Sofer

    Rena Sofer was born on December 2, 1968, in Arcadia, California, USA. As of 2023, she is 54 years old. Every year she cuts her birthday cake on the 2nd of December and her birth sign is Sagittarius. Her birth name is Rena Sherel Sofer. Her parents are Martin Sofer (father) and Susan Franzblau (mother). Rena’s father is a psychology professor while her mother worked as an Orthodox rabbi.

    Her paternal grandparents are Shammai Yoel “Joseph” Sofer and Rebecca Saliternik. David Sofer is the name of her brother. Rena has American citizenship and she belongs to Ashkenazi Jewish ethnicity. She follows the Jewish religion. She studied at the Frisch School and North Bergen High School. Sofer joined Montclair State College.

    Her first husband is an actor, Wally Kurth. They met on the set of General Hospital and started dating in 1993. Rena and Wally tied the wedding knot on the 31st March 1995. They stayed as a married couple for two years and divorced in 1997.
