    Thundurus is a Legendary Electric/Flying-type Pokémon.

    Thundurus is one of the legendary Pokémon from the Unova region. It possesses the unique combination of Electric and Flying types, making it a formidable opponent in battles.

    It is part of the Forces of Nature trio.

    Thundurus, along with Tornadus and Landorus, form the powerful Forces of Nature trio. Each Pokémon in the trio represents a different aspect of natural forces.

    Thundurus has two different forms.

    Thundurus has both an Incarnate Forme and a Therian Forme. The Incarnate Forme appears as a more aggressive and offensive version, while the Therian Forme is more focused on speed and agility.

    Its signature move is Thunderbolt.

    Thundurus’s signature move is Thunderbolt, a powerful Electric-type move that can deal massive damage to its opponents. With its high Special Attack stat, Thundurus excels at unleashing devastating Thunderbolts.

    Thundurus can create thunder clouds and cause thunderstorms.

    It is weak against Ice- and Rock-type Pokémon.

    Despite its formidable power, Thundurus has weaknesses to Ice- and Rock-type moves. Trainers who know its vulnerabilities can strategically exploit these weaknesses in battles.

    Thundurus’s appearance changes in different forms.
