    Megadeth Announces The Destroy All Enemies Tour

    06 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — The legendary thrash metal band Megadeth announced plans for a major tour, with 33 shows scheduled across North America this summer.

    Produced by Live Nation, Megadeth’s ‘Destroy All Enemies’ tour kicks off on August 2nd at Walmart AMP in Rogers, Arkansas, making stops in markets such as Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Boston, St. Louis before wrapping at Nashville’s Municipal Auditorium on September 28th.

    For the tour, Megadeth will be supported by Mudvayne and All That Remains.

    “Our ‘Crush The World’ tour has been a tremendous experience for the four of us,” shared Dave Mustaine. “We are all playing tight, and that has made it possible for me to really focus on solos and singing, we are playing more songs than ever before, and we are closer to each other, onstage AND off. I’m excited to see Mudvayne, and All That Remains. Join us as we DESTROY ALL ENEMIES.”
