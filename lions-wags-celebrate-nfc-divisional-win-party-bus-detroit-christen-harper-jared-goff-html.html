    Jared Goff's model fiance and Lions WAGs toast Detroit's playoff win with wild booze-filled party

    Christine Harper - Jared Goff's model fiance - led wives and girlfriends of the Lions into a wild booze-filled party bus to celebrate Detroit's second-round playoff win, as their NFL beaus punched their ticket to the NFC Championship game.

    25 May 2024, 00:00

    Christine Harper - Jared Goff's model fiancée - led wives and girlfriends of the Lions into a wild booze-filled party bus to celebrate Detroit's second-round playoff win, as their NFL beaus punched their ticket to the NFC Championship game. 

    Taking to Instagram, Harper shared several photos of the girl squad drinking custom Lions bottle of 1800 tequila on a party bus over the weekend. The Lions defeated the Tampa Bay Bucs', 31-23, on Sunday in the NFC Divisional round. 

    Kennedy Frazer, the fiancée of linebacker Malcolm Rodriguez; Nicole Anderson, the girlfriend of running back Jahmyr Gibbs; Lauren Wenzel, the girlfriend of linebacker Julian Okwara; Julia Raymond, the wife of Lions wide receiver Kalif Raymond; Isabelle Sewell, the wife of offensive tackle Penei Swell, were among festivities and tagged in photos with Harper. 


    They all partied hard after the Lions booked their spot for the third-round matchup postseason game for the first time in 32 years - and just the second time in franchise history.

    A Detroit influencer - Piper Perkins - even got down to do a split at a bar to celebrate the occasion, as seen in a photo shared by Wenzel. It is unclear if Perkins is dating anyone on the Lions.

    WAGS of the Detroit Lions celebrated their significant others' NFC Divisional win in a party bus

    The party bus, which was booked for Sunday, included custom Lions bottles of 1800 tequila 

    Lions WAGS were all seen with boozy drinks in their hands as raved the Lions' win over the Bucs

    Lions WAG also had a message for their significant others' opponents, the San Francisco 49ers

    Harper, far left, and other WAGS were hyped about the Lions reaching the NFC title game for the first time in 32 years

    'See you in San Fran!!' Frazer captioned her Instagram Story of WAGs sipping drinks.

    Each member of the girl squad wore some sort of Lions attire, including Harper who showed off her Lions cap while on the party bus. 

    'If you ain't first you're last,' Perkins captioned her photo of Harper with her Lions hat on, quoting Will Ferrell's comedy - 'Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby.'

    Harper also shared a snap of the sold-out crowd at Ford Field in Detroit, as a fan stood out in the photo due to his cardboard cutout of Goff as Jamie Kennedy’s character, Brad ‘B-Rad’ Gluckman, in the movie “Malibu’s Most Wanted.' 

    Harper captioned the photo with Kennedy's famous line from the 2003 classic: 'Don’t be hatin.'

    Rendon also shared a selfie of herself among other WAGS on the party bus later on Instagram

    Detroit-based influencer Piper Perkins joined the girl squad, who snagged at a table at a club at one point on Sunday night

    Harper also shared a photo of the crowd at Ford Field, where a fan held a cardboard cutout of Goff as Jamie Kennedy’s character, Brad ‘B-Rad’ Gluckman, in the 2003 movie 'Malibu's Most Wanted'

    The Lions' WAGS also held an afterparty at an undisclosed club in Detroit, as liquor bottles with firecrackers were on full display. 

    Wenzel posted a photo of bottle girls wearing Lions shirts and a lit-up sign that read: 'Lions to the Super Bowl'.

    The Lions (12-5), who are NFC North champions, will face the NFC West-leading 49ers (12-5) in San Francisco on Sunday to cement their spot in Super Bowl LVIII.

    Goff will hope to put on another flawless performance after throwing 30 of 43 for 287 yards against Tampa Bay. He threw an impressive 89-yard touchdown pass caught by Amon-Ra St. Brown, who finished the game with eight catches for 77 yards and the touchdown.
