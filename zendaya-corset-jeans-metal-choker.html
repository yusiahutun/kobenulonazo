    Zendaya Wears Large Metal Choker and Space-Age Corset with Jeans

    30 Apr 2024, 00:00

    These days, Zendaya barely gives us time to breathe before she shows up to a red carpet wearing yet another winning ensemble. Oh, and speaking of breathing, the Dune: Part Two star had us worrying for her lungs with her latest look, which she wore to the Essence Black Women in Hollywood award ceremony this afternoon.

    Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

    For the event, Zendaya and her stylist, Law Roach, pulled a look from the Jean Paul Gaultier Spring/Summer 2020 couture collection. The assemblage included a space-age silver corset that was tightly cinched around the star’s waist. This piece was decorated with metallic silver flowers, which matched the accessories all over the rest of Zendaya’s body.

    Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

    Silver was certainly the dominant theme of the outfit. Accessories-wise, the Euphoria star wore large hammered-metal cuff bracelets, skinny armbands, tiny hoop earrings, stacks of rings, and three hoops around her waist—all in silver. She also sported a thin chain belt with silver discs hanging from it that looked like the tops of metal cans.

    Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

    But one of the most attention-grabbing elements of the ensemble was Zendaya’s choker necklace, courtesy of Bulgari. The large silver accessory certainly leaned into the overall intergalactic theme, and it seemed to wink to Zendaya’s newly-released Dune film. And, while we’re not sure how comfortable it was on the Emmy-winner’s windpipe, it looked fabulous nonetheless.

    Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

    The other eye-popping element of the ’fit was those jeans, which took us back to the early aughts, when denim ruled the red carpet. For this event, Zendaya and Law styled the jeans just as they originally appeared on the runway, with the wide-leg bottoms rolled up to reveal a thick cuff. Below the pants, Zendaya sported a pair of pearly white stilettos.

    Although the Dune: Part Two press tour is now officially over, it’s good to see Zendaya isn’t slowing down any time soon.
