    h3h3 is a YouTube channel produced by an Israeli couple, Ethan and Hila Klein. It is a comedy channel on which they also post reaction videos, i.e. they find a way to “react” and satirize what is happening on the Internet. The couple also runs two other channels – a second channel called Ethan and Hila and a podcast called h3 podcast. Read on to learn more about the h3h3 YouTube channel and also the faces behind the popular Vlog.

    Ethan and Hila Klein of h3h3 – Bio, Age

    Ethan was born on June 24, 1985, in Ventura, California as Ethan Edward Klein and is one half of the h3h3Productions duo. He is the husband of Hila, who runs the station with him. Ethan has a Jewish heritage as he was born the son of Jewish parents – Gary and Donna Klein – who emigrated to the USA before he was born. He completed his high school education at Buena High School before moving to the University of California at Santa Cruz in 2004. In 2009 he graduated with a diploma in English Literature and Creative Writing.

    Hila Klein, on the other hand, was born Hila Hakmon on December 12, 1987. She also has Jewish descent and was born in Holon, Israel. And while her father is a Libyan Jew, her mother is a Turkish Jew. Hila was born into a Sephardic Jewish family and enrolled in the Shenkar College of Engineering and Design in her home country, but never completed her studies. She met her husband, Ethan Klein when she served two years in the IDF – Israeli Defense Forces. He had come to the Holocaust Museum in Jerusalem, and it was there that their paths crossed.

    Career – h3h3 YouTube Channel

    The h3h3 YouTube channel debuted in 2011 and its content is mainly reaction videos. There are videos on the channel that criticize other Internet entities and even some guidelines adopted by the social media platform itself – YouTube. When the channel debuted in 2011, its first postings were videos of Hila.

    H3h3Productions has become so popular on the Internet that it currently has over 5.7 million subscribers (as of 2018) and more than 1 billion viewers. The station’s best-viewed video to date is the video “Vape Nation”, which was uploaded in March 2016. By May 2018, the video has recorded over 22 million views, and the trend is rising.

    His other popular videos include The Fine Bros Rant, How to kiss – h3h3 reaction video, Kissing Pranks. The YouTube couple launched a podcast in 2017, which they called H3 Podcast, where they interview other YouTube personalities and celebrities and the videos are uploaded twice a week. Celebrities who have appeared on the H3 podcast include Justin Roiland (who appeared in their first podcast), Post Malone, Jake Paul, Bob Saget, Pewdiepie, Bo Burnham, Jordan Peterson, Ninja, and so on.

    Net Worth – How Much is h3h3 Worth?

    With a subscriber base of over 5 million (5.7 million at last count) and an estimated 1 million views per day of all the channel’s content, Ethan and Hila (h3h3) would earn an estimated $1,800 per day, which adds up to a combined annual income of $660,000.

    If you add this to the income the couple earns from selling h3h3 branded t-shirts and other items on the h3h3 website, as well as the ads placed on the uploaded videos, the net worth is $1.5 million.
