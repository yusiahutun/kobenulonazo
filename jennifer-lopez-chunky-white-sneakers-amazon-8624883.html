    If there’s one thing we can count on when it comes to Jennifer Lopez’s style, it’s consistency. Case in point: her affinity for chunky white sneakers. The A-lister has been wearing the controversial shoe trend for years, and over the past few weeks, Lopez wore the same pair of Prada Cloudbust Thunder sneakers four times. Since the shoes are $1,200, I took the liberty of finding a $36 lookalike pair that’s available on Amazon. 

    Last month, J.Lo posted a video on Instagram wearing a pink workout set and the chunky Prada sneakers. And just this past week, she was spotted three times wearing the shoes in New York City; first with leggings and a cropped jacket, then with a casual light gray sweat suit, and finally with a dramatic faux fur coat. 

    Like Lopez’s go-to Prada sneakers, the Dream Pairs chunky style have geometric details featured throughout their mesh upper and lug soles. They also have cushioned insoles for optimal comfort and a pull tab on the back for easy on-and-off. While the white version most closely resembles Lopez’s pair, the shoes come in four additional colorways. 

    Dream Pairs Chunky Platform Sneakers

    Still need more convincing to give the shoes a try? Take it from hundreds of Amazon shoppers who have given them a five-star rating. One person called them their “go-to shoes since they’re so comfy and they match basically anything.” Someone else said they’re “very lightweight” despite their chunky look, and a third person gets “so many compliments on them.” 

    Other reviewers shared how they style the sneakers. One shopper said the shoes go well with “cute summer dresses and also look amazing with activewear.” And another person likes to wear the Dream Pairs sneakers with “cargo [and] track pants” — another Jennifer Lopez-approved fashion trend.
