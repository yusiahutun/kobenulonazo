    Quibblo is a social networking site where you can create and take fun quizzes and surveys, view results on a chart and compare your answers to others'. You can also search for quizzes or browse by subject categories (e.g. 2008 Election, American Idol, Dumb, Funny, Celebrity and so on), by tags and check out most popular quizzes on the site.

    While most quizzes and polls are taken on the site, Quibblo quizzes can be easily added to your profile on MySpace, Facebook etc. or your Wordpress / Blogger blog. Check out the demo video below.

    Features:

    • Free quizzes, polls and surveys created by users.
    • Create your own quizzes or take quizzes by other members, view results on graph charts and compare your answers to others'.
    • Search for quizzes or browse by tags and by subject categories (e.g. 2008 Election, American Idol, Dumb, Funny, Celebrity).
    • View popular and top rated quizzes.
    • Quizzes can be embedded into your favorite social networks and platforms including MySpace, Facebook, Wordpress, Blogger and others.
    • Free, view and take quizzes without registration. Register to create your own quizzes.
    • Similar sites: MyStuidiyo, ClassMarker and QToro.

    Check out Quibblo @ www.quibblo.com
