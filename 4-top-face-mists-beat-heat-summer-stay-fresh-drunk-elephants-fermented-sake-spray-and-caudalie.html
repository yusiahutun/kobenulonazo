    4 top face mists to beat the heat this summer: stay fresh with Drunk Elephant’s fermented sake spray and Caudalie Beauty Elixir, or try hydrating skincare hacks from Tatcha and Dr Barbara Sturm

    Drying out in the heat? Your skin needs some instant hydration, stat – ideally delivered with one of these refreshingly cool face mists.

    Whether you’re in need of a quick complexion boost or just looking to help your make-up set, we’ve got you covered. Check out four of our favourite face mists, from skincare labels such as Caudalie and Dr Barbara Sturm, below.

    Summer ready! 6 new must-have beauty and skincare products to buy in June

    1. Caudalie Beauty Elixir – US$20/30ml

    This radiance-boosting eau de beauté is a refreshingly lightweight mist and toner that soothes the skin, tightens pores and sets make-up. It’s no wonder celebrities such as Victoria Beckham are fans.

    2. Dr Barbara Sturm Hydrating Face Mist – US$100/50ml

    Suitable for all skin types, this mist will give dull and tired-looking complexions a much-needed boost. It contains ingredients such as hyaluronic acid and extracts from lemon, aloe vera and broccoli.

    3. Drunk Elephant Sweet Biome Fermented Sake Spray – US$42/100ml

    The American ‘clean’ skincare brand’s spray is designed to stimulate collagen production and strengthen the skin’s barrier. Use it with any of the brand’s serums, treatments or moisturisers.

    4. Tatcha Luminous Dewy Skin Mist – US$54/40ml

    Formulated for dry textures, this mist is enriched with botanical oils, humectants and the label’s proprietary Hadasei-3 complex to lock in moisture and combat visible signs of ageing.
