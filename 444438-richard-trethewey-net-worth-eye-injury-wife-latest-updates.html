    Richard Trethewey is an HVAC specialist specializing in heating and plumbing. He is a licensed master plumber in Massachusetts, where he practices his trade. He is a fourth-generation plumber who learned the trade from his father.

After migrating from Nova Scotia, he settled in Boston, setting up a plumbing business - Trethewey Brothers - with his brother. The business grew into a full-fledged plumbing and heating company, Trethewey Brothers Inc., which Richard’s father (Ron Trethewey) and his uncle (John Trethewey) inherited.
