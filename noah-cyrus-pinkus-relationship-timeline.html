    23 Apr 2024, 00:00

    After two months of openly dating, Noah Cyrus and Pinkus are set to be married.

    On Monday, Cyrus announced on Instagram that she’s been engaged to the German fashion designer for weeks. “This past month of being your fiancé and being in our own little world of just us two has been so perfect and i wish i could never leave this time,” the singer wrote. “I never thought i would ever meet a man like you or someone so selfless and filled with so much love to give. you’re the least judge mental human being i’ve ever met. the most talented, the most loyal, the kindest person. i’ve never felt more loved or in love.”

    It remains unclear how exactly Cyrus and her husband-to-be first met, or when they officially began dating—but since April, they have been sprinkling sweet glimpses of their romance all over social media (and have not been shy about expressing their affection in each other’s comment sections).

    Ahead, see everything we know so far about the newly engaged couple.

    They went public on TikTok.

    Cyrus confirmed her new romance with the designer via a TikTok video she shared on April 16. In the clip, which features her own song with Vance Joy “Everybody Needs Someone,” she and Pinkus are seen in a parked car gazing lovingly into each other’s eyes and sharing a sweet kiss. “For once, my happiness is leaking into my music,” Cyrus wrote in text over the video. “Thank you.”

    They support one another in fashion.

    Pinkus’s vocation has evidently rubbed off on the singer. This past week, Cyrus walked her first-ever fashion show with Marine Serre—an occasion where she also debuted her diamond engagement ring.

    “What an honor it is for me to have walked my first show for you,” Cyrus wrote in an Instagram post featuring a shot of her walking the runway, alongside other moments from show. “This entire experience was so loving and positive and i’m forever grateful for everyone involved… my heartbeats for @marineserre_official … thank you thank you thank you i love you so much.”

    Pinkus posted about the proposal on his own page after the show, a day prior to Cyrus’s official engagement announcement. “Proudest fiancé,” he captioned the carousel, which started with a shot of Cyrus on the runway, and was followed by a zoomed-in screenshot of the singer’s hand, showcasing her dazzling new ring.

    Cyrus is also a proud supporter of Pinkus’s work. The designer—who founded the clothing company Colors—recently flaunted Cyrus in a carousel of blurry, grungy photos of the singer modeling several of his designs. “Garage webcam,” he captioned the slideshow of looks.

    “I love you,” Cyrus commented on the post. (She later circled back to write, “I MISSSS YOU SO MUCH COME HOME.”)

    They regularly coordinate their looks.

    Since getting together, the two have been photographed on multiple occasions mirroring one another in edgy goth ’fits. Such was the case on their recent trip to Paris, where they coordinated in dark ensembles with headpieces.

    Over the weekend, Pinkus shared a carousel of shots from the trip on his Instagram, and Cyrus wrote in the comments, “My everything.”
