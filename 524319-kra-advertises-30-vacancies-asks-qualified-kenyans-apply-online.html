    KRA Advertises Over 35 Vacancies, Asks Qualified Kenyans to Apply Online

    The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) has advertised over 35 vacancies as it embarks on a tax collection drive to meet its revenue collection target. KRA asked interested and qualified Kenyans to apply for the vacant positions through its online recruitment portal.

    The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) has advertised over 35 vacancies as it embarks on a tax collection drive to meet its revenue collection target.

    KRA asked interested and qualified Kenyans to apply for the vacant positions through its online recruitment portal.

    The government's principal revenue collector said the deadline for making the applications was October 29, except for the position of assistant manager-knowledge management infrastructure(strategy innovation and risk management department) set for October 31.

    "Kenya Revenue Authority does not charge any fee at any stage of the recruitment process (application, shortlisting, interviewing, and/or offer.

    What are qualifications for graduate trainees?

    At the same time, KRA advertised vacancies for graduate trainees who will be nurtured into tax administration experts.

    They will be tasked with administrating relevant tax and customs laws and enforcing compliance.

    In a notice, KRA noted that the programme targets university graduates aged 28 years and below.
