    Personal Life: Boyfriend And Dating Rumor

    Erin had been rumored to be dating her Destination Truth co-star Josh Gates since 2010. Their on-screen chemistry was loved and admired by fans to the extent that they assumed the two were romantic partners in real life.

    However, in April 2019, the gossip came to an end when Josh’s marital status surfaced out. Even Erin eradicated the rumor by making a tweet on 11th April 2019. Erin tweeted by saying that Josh was happily married to the mother of his two babies.

    Career Information

    Erin began working from 2003 as a production assistant in sports at NBC Olympics Division. In the company, she served for one year.

    After a year, she joined ESPN and worked as a producer for one month at Aspen. Again, she went to the Greater Los Angeles Area and worked for the same company in the same position for a month in 2005. 

    In September 2005, Erin joined Viacom and fulfilled her duties as a producer until July 2006. Then, she served for 10x10 Productions and worked from 2007 till 2008 as a senior producer. 

    Later, Erin worked for Ping-Pong Production as a co-executive producer for almost four years from May 2008 to July 2012. After that, she went to Zoo Productions and served as the supervising producer.
