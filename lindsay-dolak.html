    Title: Contributing Writer

    Education: Syracuse University

    Location: New York, New York

    Expertise: Weddings, Beauty, Fashion

    • Lindsay Dolak is a writer and editor based in New York who covers all things weddings, beauty, and fashion.
    • She was an editor for InStyle for two years, first as an editorial assistant and then working her way up to Assistant Editor. She wrote and edited regular articles for the print magazine and produced e-commerce content for the digital version.
    • She is currently the Senior Content Manager for Zola.

    Experience

    Lindsay began her career on the print and digital sides of the magazine industry, where she produced 360 content strategies for brands that include direction for relevant social channels, editorial, and SEO development. Her experience includes writing and editing at Terakeet, InStyle, and Aaptiv. She has been the Senior Content Manager for Zola for the past three years.

    Education

    Lindsay has her Bachelor's degree in Magazine Journalism from Syracuse University.
