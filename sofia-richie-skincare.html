    23 Products She Uses For Sensitive Skin

    I recently wrote about Sofia Richie’s makeup and exposed my TikTok crush on her. My beauty inspo looks beautiful no matter which products she puts on her face, but behind her signature, minimal makeup aesthetic is a good skincare routine. After digging and digging, I can confirm that Sofia’s favorite skincare products are equally as amazing as her makeup picks.

    Why? Because a lot of them come from a line that her dermatologist of 10+ years formulated, as well as the popular beauty brand NUDESTIX (for which the newlywed serves as its first-ever beauty director). But LANCER Skincare and NUDESTIX aside, she doesn’t shy away from using affordable options such as an under-$10 Mario Badescu facial spray or the viral, fan-fave Summer Fridays Lip Butter Balm. On the other end of the price spectrum, the model splurges on fancy formulas like Chanel’s eye masks and The LYMA Laser that “transformed” her skin in one week.

    But let’s backtrack a tiny bit. Sofia has shared that she has sensitive and oily skin, so she is certainly no stranger to calming products. Avene’s offerings are known for soothing, hydrating, and restoring sensitive skin, so it comes as no surprise that she keeps several of them on her skincare tray. It’s also unsurprising that the model has added Tatcha’s blotting papers to her arsenal while pregnant.

    If these highlights alone are making you want to start shopping Sofia Richie’s skincare routine, get the full picture below with all 23 products she uses.

    • HATCH Belly Oil

      HATCH Belly Oil

      In a TikTok going over maternity favorites, Sofia says she is on her second bottle of HATCH’s Belly Oil . The brand’s best-seller offers head-to-toe hydration and doesn’t leave behind a greasy residue, per the model.

    • Summer Fridays Lip Butter Balm

      Summer Fridays Lip Butter Balm

      In the same maternity faves TikTok, Sofia swears she’s “addicted” to the new Birthday Cake shade of Summer Friday’s viral, fan-favorite Lip Butter Balm. She also says in a wedding weekend GRWM TikTok, that she’s “obsessed” with the balm. Birthday Cake is the prettiest light pink color with a touch of shimmer. While it might be sold out at Sephora, you can still order it on Revolve.

    • Tatcha Blotting Papers

      Tatcha Aburatorigami Japanese Blotting Papers

      “Literally I cannot function without them ,” Sofia says in the same maternity faves TikTok. “My skin has been so oily ever since I found out I was pregnant, so I travel around with these and I just like blot my skin. It kind of like helps me not having 20 layers of powder on my face by the end of the day, so these are a lifesaver.”

    • LYMA Laser

      Another skincare product the model “cannot function without” is The LYMA Laser. “I cannot say enough good things about it. It is The LYMA Laser. I do it every night before bed like when I’m watching a movie. This has changed my skin,” she said in the maternity faves TikTok, adding that, “Within a week, this transformed my skin.”

    • SkinCeuticals Bundle

      SkinCeuticals recently announced Sofia Richie as a global brand partner, and with that, a bundle curated by Dr. Mamina Turegano, a triple board-certified aesthetic dermatologist and SkinCeuticals brand partner. While the kit comes with five products for $541, if you want to buy individual products separately, here’s everything that’s included in the bundle:

    • Avene Cicalfate Cream

      Avene Cicalfate+ Restorative Protective Cream

      Sofia spent a day with Vogue and none other than the Avene Cicalfate+ cream can be spotted in her skincare setup. It’s best for maintaining a healthy skin barrier and hydrating the complexion. However, the model and influencer isn’t the only “it” girl who loves this sensitive skin-friendly formula. Hailey Bieber reaches for it whenever her skin feels stressed out.

    • Mario Badescu Facial Spray

      Mario Badescu Facial Spray with Aloe, Herbs and Rose Water

      Don’t underestimate the power of a good face mist. Whenever your skin feels dry and in need of a pick-me-up, simply spritz Mario Badescu’s rose water spray onto your face, like how Sofia does in this GRWM video.

    • Chanel Eye Masks

      Chanel Le Lift Firming – Anti-Wrinkle Flash Eye Revitalizer

      If you’ve been following Sofia for a while now (and watched her Vogue Beauty Secrets), you’ll know she’s a Chanel girl through and through—even down to her eye masks! These bougie hydrogel ones wake up your under-eyes, and the set comes with 10 pairs of masks.

    • Avene Thermal Water Spray

      Avene Thermal Water Spray

      On Sale 9% off

      Calm sensitive skin with Avene’s Thermal Water Spray , which appeared on Sofia’s vanity in this YouTube video. Get it in the $10 travel size so you can take it with you on the go and refresh your complexion throughout the day.

    • Supergoop Glow Stick

      Supergoop! Glow Stick SPF 50

      Not a day should go by when you’re not protecting your skin with sunscreen. The newlywed uses a Supergoop! stick in this YouTube GRWM and gently pats it on her skin. It makes Sofia’s complexion look glowy (it’s literally in the product’s name) instead of greasy.

    • Avene Face Cream

      Avene XeraCalm A.D Lipid-Replenishing Cream

      Last but certainly not least for Avene, the model keeps this replenishing cream at hand in this Vogue video. The formula restores the skin’s barrier and offers up to 48 hours of hydration. If you have dry, itchy skin, this face cream might be your answer.

    • La Mer Eye Concentrate

      La Mer’s Eye Concentrate is by far Sofia’s priciest skincare essential. She layers it underneath her Chanel hydrogel patches in this GRWM. Together, she says these products help tackle her under-eye dark circles.

    • EltaMD Facial Moisturizer

      EltaMD AM Therapy Facial Moisturizer

      Sofia told Byrdie that this daytime EltaMD face moisturizer is an “old favorite.” It’s sheer and non-comedogenic, and it also promotes cell turnover, evens skin tone, and boosts moisture retention.

    • NUDESTIX Face Cleanser

      NUDESTIX Citrus Clean Balm & Makeup Melt

      Sofia revealed to Byrdie that she likes to keep her daytime and nighttime skincare routines pretty simple. In the morning, she washes her face with this cleansing balm from NUDESTIX. What makes this formula so special is that it has four different functions: makeup remover, skin cleanser, skin softener, and skin brightener.

    • NUDESTIX Gel Face Cleanser

      NUDESTIX Gentle Hydra-Gel Face Cleanser

      I know Sofia is a huge supporter of NUDESTIX’s makeup (plus she’s the brand’s first-ever beauty director), but she also has a ton of faves from its skincare offerings (read more in her interview with Byrdie). You saw the cleansing balm above, but make sure to also check out the brand’s gel face cleanser. Per the brand, “It is developed with vegan-powered actives for the most sensitive eyes and skin to reduce the appearance of irritation, blemishes, and dryness.”

    • NUDESTIX Mask and Moisturizer

      NUDESTIX NUDESKIN Citrus-C Mask & Daily Moisturizer

      Follow Sofia’s lead and add this vitamin C-packed face moisturizer to your daily regimen. It helps brighten, increase skin elasticity, and even skin tone.

    • NUDESTIX Sunscreen

      NUDESTIX Nudescreen Daily Mineral Face Veil SPF 30

      “The NUDESCREEN Daily Mineral SPF 30 is my go-to, it’s lightweight, and the warm shade adds a slight tint to my skin,” the model told Byrdie.

    • NUDESTIX Lip Butter

      NUDESTIX Nudeskin Hydra-Peptide Lip Butter

      While Sofia is obsessed with Chanel’s tinted balm, a more affordable lip product is the Hydra-Peptide Lip Butter. “I always have one of these in my bag. It is so moisturizing and leaves a glossy, plump look that mimics the appearance of wearing a lip gloss,” she shared with Byrdie.

    • NUDESTIX Micro-Peel

      NUDESTIX NUDESKIN Lemon-Aid Detox & Glow Micro-Peel

      The final and most recent NUDESTIX skincare product in Sofia’s rotation is this micro-peel. She told Byrdie, “This product is super gentle and leaves my skin smooth and radiant.”

    • LANCER Skincare Face Cleanser

      $54.99608% Off

      In this Vogue Beauty Secrets video, Sofia reveals that she’s been seeing Dr. Harold Lancer since she was 13 years old, and he is her family’s trusted dermatologist. So it comes as no surprise that she relies on her derm’s skincare line. First up is this face cleanser that contains rice-silk proteins, amino acids, and aloe extract to cleanse and hydrate.

    • LANCER Skincare Moisturizer

      LANCER Skincare The Method: Nourish Women’s Anti-Aging Moisturizer

      This nourishing moisturizer mixes peptides, hyaluronic acid, avocado oil, and olive fruit oil to leave your complexion looking youthful and glowy. It’s another LANCER Skincare pick from Sofia’s Vogue GRWM.

    • LANCER Skincare Skin Perfector

      LANCER Skincare Dani Glowing Skin Perfector

      The last up from her derm’s line is the Dani Glowing Skin Perfector . No wonder why her face always looks so dewy and radiant. Mix it with your foundation, wear it alone, or use it as a highlighter. Sofia primes her complexion with it in this YouTube video.

    • Rodial Glow Booster Drops

      Sometimes one glowy product isn’t enough. After the LANCER skin perfector, she goes in with another illuminating product in the same video: Rodial’s Rodial Soft Focus Glow Booster Drops . It creates the perfect base for the rest of your makeup and adds a “lit-from-within” radiance to your face.
