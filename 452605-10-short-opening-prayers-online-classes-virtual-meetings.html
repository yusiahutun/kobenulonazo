    Virtual learning has been a concept for quite a while. However, the spread of Covid 19 saw an increase in online classes around the world. Campuses in different parts of the globe encouraged students to sign up for online classes, as physical classes were cancelled. The online learning programs are continuing and have even made learning more convenient. With just a laptop and stable internet, you can learn whatever course you want. For believers, opening prayers for online classes is crucial and becomes a part of their routine before starting the class.

    Prayers are like appeals to God. Through prayer, you talk to your creator, asking him to bless you and guide you in whatever you are doing. If you are a student, make it a habit to start your online class with prayers for the online class. The prayers will give you confidence and will to sit through the class and grasp everything being taught.

    Opening prayers for online classes

    How do you pray to start a class? Think of the program being taught and ask God to give you understanding. Also, make the prayer short for the ease of everyone.

    Short opening prayer before a class

    Making a short prayer before the class takes nothing away from you. On the contrary, prayer gives you fulfilment. You can ask for permission before every class and pray aloud before the class officially begins.

    Dear God, we humbly gather together virtually. Thinking of starting today's online class. Oh, father, we come to you asking for your guidance, wisdom, and support as we wait to be pumped with knowledge by our teachers. Please help us understand whatever we are being taught, so we can have a brighter future when we finish our program. We pray this trusting your holy name. Amen.

    English prayer for an online class

    English is a universal language and thus easy to communicate in. Make a short opening prayer for the online class before your class starts, and ask God for everything you need. Ask God to send the spirit of understanding and to make the learning session satisfying for you.

    God the Father, God the Son, and God the holy spirit. I come before you this morning grateful for the gift of life. I am thankful for having a good night's sleep and waking up feeling fresh. Dear God, I am about to start my online class. May you send your spirit down here so that I may understand what is being taught. Remember my classmates too. We are asking you to guide our minds and hearts throughout the online session. Help us concentrate during the class and give us good grades at the end of the semester.
