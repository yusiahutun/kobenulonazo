    It's all about Balmain!

    Young Hollywood's hottest faces stepped out to support H&M's latest designer collaboration with French fashion house Balmain on Wednesday in West Hollywood.

    The Olivier Rousteing-designed duds have proved to be extremely popular with A-list fashionistas. Models Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner are even featured in the line's campaign, giving the designs even more of a cool-girl appeal!

    Sarah Hyland looked chic at the event in a patterned shift dress from the collabo. She kept her lob in loose waves and paired the look with a funky belt and black booties.

    Dylan Penn was flawless as she rocked a head-to-toe look from the designer as well. The model showed off her curves in a black and red color block mini paired with a wide gold belt. She kept her hair in a high ponytail and completed the look with thigh high boots and a black clutch!

    And Ashley Benson wowed in warm tones with pieces from H&M's A/W 2015 Studio Collection. The "Pretty Little Liars" star paired a billowy turtle neck top with a pair of burnt orange high-waisted pants. She finished the look with gold pumps, a red lip and a half up bun hairstyle.

    Michael Kovac

    But they weren't the only celebs who were flawless at the event! Mother-daughter duo Cindy Crawford and Kaia Gerber looked fierce in the designs.

    The 14-year-old model-in-training chose a beaded and embellished graphic print dress, while her mama donned an over-sized blazer, leather pants and a black tee. Both ladies looked gorgeous with natural makeup and tousled waves.

    Celebs got to exclusively shop the collection at the VIP event before it launched, allowing them to get their hands on the coveted pieces. On Thursday, shoppers went insane over the product at stores around the country -- and the collection is already sold out online!
