    I'm not really here to throw shade at drugstore shampoos, but investing in a higher-quality formula will improve the vitality of your strands by tenfold. Sure, it can be easy to avert your eyes from ingredient lists and instead focus on affordable pricing, but treating your scalp half as well as you treat your skin can result in healthier, stronger locks that are less prone to thinning and breakage.

    While it's true some factors that contribute to hair loss, including genetics, can be more difficult to rectify than others, taking small steps, such as implementing antioxidant-rich formulas into your regimen can help promote a healthy scalp. Case in point: the Rene Furterer Forticea Energizing Shampoo. Made to re-energize stressed-out scalps and encourage stronger hair follicles from the roots to the ends, the anti-loss formulation deeply cleanses with each wash while delivering vital nutrients to areas of concern.

    Forticea Energizing 1.6-Ounce Shampoo

    You'll find top-notch ingredients within the shampoo to nourish strands, such as natural guarana extract, which is packed with caffeine to stimulate ample hair growth and stunt the effects of DHT (a hormone that plays a role in hair loss). Additionally, orange, lavender, and rosemary essential oils detoxify and cleanse any build-up on the scalp, a critical step since dead skin, sweat, and oil can clog hair follicles and hinder growth. As professional hairdresser Carrera Alvarez previously shared with InStyle, "Hair growth isn't just about treating the hair that has grown out, it's about what is happening on the scalp. In fact, learning how to treat your scalp is the first step to preventing hair loss."

    If you're experiencing shedding, thinning, or hair loss, you're not alone. According to the American Hair Loss Association, 40 percent of hair loss sufferers are women. Implementing products to encourage a healthy scalp environment and being consistent with your hair care routine is a step you can take towards improved strands. And, if you're skeptical of the Rene Furterer Forticea Energizing Shampoo, results are truly possible from using it; according to a three-week brand conducted study, 60 percent of users claimed they experienced fewer broken hairs and lusher tresses.

    Forticea Energizing 6.7-Ounce Shampoo


    Need more proof? Just click over to the customer reviews section, where shoppers on the Rene Furterer site sing its praises. One shopper has relied on the shampoo for years after it was recommended by their hairstylist, sharing that their hair is now "healthy, shiny, and soft." Another joked that the product is "one of the reasons" their marriage is still intact after it helped their husband's hair grow.

    "After years of using everything I could to make my thinning fine hair look fuller and healthy, I found this and it's a miracle," wrote a third reviewer. "I can let my hair air dry and it's full, shiny and has body and movement. Please never discontinue this!"

    "I heard about this fancy-looking french shampoo from my friend who recently went to Paris," said an equally pleased Amazon reviewer. "It was a bit pricey for me, since I have long hair and I would use it every day. However, I really like using this shampoo and I have less hair falling out after washing my hair. The key point to remember is that you have to wait for three minutes before rinsing."
