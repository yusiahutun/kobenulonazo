    Larsa Pippen is taking all the due credit for her taking care of her body, and claims that her curvaceous physique is a result of constantly working out. She is also shutting down rumors about having gotten a butt lift.

    At the reunion of Season 4 'Real Housewives of Miami' during a conversation with TV host, Andy Cohen, she admitted that she had indeed gotten her nose and lips corrected professionally, but not her posterior. She said, "I’ve had my nose done. I’ve had my lips done. And that’s basically it".

    In addition, Pippin also revealed that she had her breasts augmented, but not before getting ultra-defensive about her bottom. She says that her butt is perfectly shaped solely because of her intense workout regime, and nothing else. 

    During her talk with Cohen about her transformation, Pippin shared a little about her tiresome workout schedule, saying, "I literally work out seven days a week". What's more, credits to her now voluptuous figure go to her intense weight and muscle training, thereby explaining how she looked a lot thinner in her older pictures. 

    Pippen said, "If I show you my photos from five years ago, I was less than 100 pounds. I’m 140 pounds now, so yeah, my legs look thicker than they were, my arms look thicker than they were. My whole body has changed … My body’s tight because I work out".

    Having said that, Pippen said that she isn't against plastic surgery, although she completely denied the butt lift remark. She's a strong believer in doing whatever makes her feel best and comfortable. She says, "I feel like I’m very progressive. I’m into trends and I feel like whatever makes me feel good and look good, I’m willing to do. I’m that person", adding, "I’m very happy with the way I look". 

    Sometime last month, Pippen cooked up more drama on the show. in one of the episodes of 'Real Housewives of Miami', she explained why she and her now ex-best friend Kim Kardashian had a major falling out.

    Pippen stated that their friendship came to an abrupt end because she "knew too much" about Kim and her currently estranged husband Kanye West's drama. Sounds like a hoot indeed. 

    In her defense, Pippen explained that she was indeed really close to the family and hence, would bear witness to what was really happening at the Kardashians', thereby making her 'a problem'. She added, "I took a beating because I was the friend that was basically there and saw everything and that basically was the demise of our relationship. I knew too much, I was a problem and so whatever. That's kind of what happened".
