    They say, “There is art in every heart!” I am sure you are also one of those who feel pumped up after listening to a rousing BGM, cry silently after watching an emotional movie, dance their heart out at a family wedding, and get nostalgic after reading a coming-of-age story. Rightfully so, art takes you on a journey where you experience different emotions to their fullest. To take you on that journey again,

    We have come up with 20 quotes on art that will appease the art lover within you. 

    20 Best Quotes on Art 

    1. “Artists are just children who refuse to put down their crayons.”- Al Hirschfeld

    2. “Something is always born of excess; great art was born of great terrors, great loneliness, great inhibitions, and it always balances them.”- Anais Nin

    3. “Learn the rules like a pro, so you can break them like an artist.”- Pablo Picasso

    4. “Painting is poetry that is seen rather than felt, and poetry is a painting that is felt rather than seen.”- Leonardo da Vinci

    5. “Eleanor was right. She never looked nice. She looked like art, and art wasn’t supposed to look nice; it was supposed to make you feel something.”- Rainbow Rowell

    6. “I dream painting and I paint my dream.”- Vincent Van Gogh

    7. “If you ask me what I came to do in this world, I, an artist will answer you: I am here to live out loud.”- Émile Zola

    8. “One ought, every day at least, to hear a little song, read a good poem, see a fine picture, and if it were possible, to speak a few reasonable words.”- Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

     9. “Poetry surrounds us everywhere, but putting it on paper is, alas, not so easy as looking at it.”- Vincent Van Gogh

    10. “The aim of art is to represent not the outward appearance of things, but their inward significance.”- Aristotle

    11. “An intellectual says a simple thing in a hard way. An artist says a hard thing in a simple way.”- Charles Bukowski

    12. “Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still.”- Dorothea Lange

    13. “Art is never finished, only abandoned.”- Leonardo da Vinci

    14. “Art is standing with one hand extended into the universe and one hand extended into the world, and letting ourselves be a conduit for passing energy.”- Albert Einstein

    15. “Everything you can imagine is real.”- Pablo Picasso

    16. “Art deteriorates when it is done for the audience.”- James Pierce

    17. “In any art you’re allowed to steal anything if you can make it better.”- Ernest Hemingway

    18. “Art enables us to find ourselves and lose ourselves at the same time.”- Thomas Merton

    19. “You have a million excuses. Write anyway.”- Carrie Kei Heim

    20. “Art is science made clear.”- Jean Cocteau

    Pablo Picasso, one of the most celebrated artists of the generation, once quoted, “Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist when he/she grows up.” We hope these 20 quotes on art will inspire you to discover and nurture the artist within you!
