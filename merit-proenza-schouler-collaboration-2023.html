    After once again partnering for New York Fashion Week, Merit and Proenza Schouler have taken their ongoing collaboration to the next level, releasing a reversible leather handbag inspired by Merit’s Signature Bag (which comes complimentary with your first beauty purchase from the brand). Part makeup bag, part Proenza clutch, the black and dusty-blue pouch ships free with every Merit order over $100 while supplies last.

    “[Proenza Schouler founders] Jack McCollough and Lazaro Hernandez have played a leading role in defining American sportswear for the last two decades, and their ability to take luxury and balance it with ease and wearability is intrinsically aligned with what we do at Merit,” Merit senior vice president of brand growth and innovation Aila Morin told Bazaar in an exclusive. “Their reimagining of the Signature Bag—its versatility, relaxed shape, and elevated yet practical material—speaks to our shared brand vision.”

    Shop

    Daniel Shea for MERIT

    The companies have been working together behind the scenes for years, so it was only a matter of time before a new collaboration came to be. In fact, Morin added, styles from Proenza Schouler have been on Merit’s internal mood boards since before the beauty brand even launched in 2021. Plus, what makeup lover could forget when Merit’s Signature Lip made its official debut on Proenza Schouler’s Fall/Winter 2022 runway? The two modern luxury brands are a match made in heaven.

    Astrid Stawiarz//Getty Images

    Models pose backstage during Proenza Schouler’s Fall/Winter 2022 runway show with Merit’s Signature Lip.

    Trend-spotting site Firstfinds predicts the designer makeup back won’t stay in stock for long based on how quickly the exclusive Merit x Proenza Schouer runway set (released last month for preorder) went out of stock on Moda Operandi. You can re-create the sold-out Merit x Proenza Schouler makeup set by adding five bestsellers to your cart while the limited-edition bag is still available: the brand’s Great Skin glow serum, creamy Flush Balm cheek color, Clean Lash mascara, Shade Slick tinted lip oil, and No. 1 Brush for a flawless finish.

    While the Merit x Proenza Schouler bag is designed to house the lineup for your five-minute face, it’s stylish enough to take just about anywhere. “I love this set because it’s built for speed,” Moda Operandi beauty director Jessica Matlin said of the new collaboration. “These neutral products are chic, creamy, and super easy to apply on the go. I’ve literally applied the blush running to the Moda office.”

    The limited-edition bag won’t be in stock for long, so you’ll need to finalize your purchase fast to ensure you get yours. Only customers spending $100 or more on their orders will receive the new bag as a gift-with-purchase, so consider it an added incentive to restock your fall makeup essentials. First purchases under $100 will still ship with the original Signature Bag.

    The Merit x Proenza Schouler Makeup Bag is available as a free gift-with-purchase on orders over $100 at meritbeauty.com while supplies last.
