    How to open Equity Bank diaspora account

    As the world narrows and becomes more like a village, so have the banking sectors enabled their customers to access their services across borders. Equity Bank diaspora account offers a range of services to suit the banking requirements of individuals working and living abroad.

    03 Apr 2024, 00:00

    As the world narrows and becomes more like a village, so have the banking sectors enabled their customers to access their services across borders. Equity Bank diaspora account offers a range of services to suit the banking requirements of individuals working and living abroad.

    If you are working abroad, Equity Bank has made it possible for you to benefit from its service through Equity Bank diaspora account. Among the service one enjoys are:

    • High-quality service tailored to suit your needs
    • A 24-hour contact Centre to handle any queries
    • A professional and dedicated staff at your disposal and
    • Quick resolution to most of your questions

    Equity Bank diaspora

    Equity Bank enables you to bank globally as you work globally. Through their Kenyan shillings accounts or foreign currency accounts, Customers are presented with options from which they can choose accounts that suit their particular needs.

    The Kenya Shillings accounts options are; the Diaspora Easy save, Jijenge member account, Junior member account, Call/Fixed account, Diaspora current account, Diaspora business account, and Diaspora current business account.

    How to open Equity Bank diaspora account

    Once you have an Equity Bank account, you can transact conveniently anywhere on the globe. As their customer, you can transact through your phone, Equity Bank online platform or using an Equity Bank card.

    How to open via email or post

    When opening the diaspora account through email, you will be required to attach documents such as

    • Passport size photo
    • Passport copy
    • Utility bill (to verify your location)and
    • A duly filled and signed indemnity form

    • Scanned Kenyan Identification card or valid passport
    • Utility bill documents, for example, Electricity bill or driving license

    Equity Bank diaspora loans

    The bank has extended its credit facilities to customers living abroad. The service can be provided until completion while you are elsewhere.

    Among the loan services available to customers are;

    • Diaspora mortgage/ Construction loans: Helps you access funds towards the purchase of a house or construction of residential or commercial buildings.
    • Asset Finance: An investment that will enable you to purchase a business asset, for example, a vehicle, equipment or machine
    • Business Loans: These are secure loans for business growth or expansion of an already existing business.
    • Personal loans:These are secured and unsecured loans accessed by the employed individuals based on their employment contracts.
    • Trade Finance: You can get bid bonds, performance bonds, LPO financing, advance payment guarantees, letter of credit and bank guarantees, which suites different business requirements.
    • Plot purchase loans: Enables you to borrow funds to help in buying plots.
