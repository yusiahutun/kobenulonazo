07 Jul 2024, 00:00

As modern brides increasingly favour alternative styles of engagement rings over traditional solitaires, the toi et moi has taken centre stage. For those unfamiliar, the toi et moi – “you and me” in French – refers to an unusual style of ring set with two complementary gemstones side-by-side on a coiled or twisted band, representing the union of two lovers as one. Such designs traditionally feature twinned white diamonds, but have also been set throughout history with coloured gemstones of varying cuts and colours.
