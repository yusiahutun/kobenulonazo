    Money-making apps allow you to work in your free time through a simple sign-up process while receiving fast and easy payment. Legit apps to earn money will not cost you anything upfront and are free to download. But others charge you a fee, which they deduct from your earnings.

    Ideally, the money you make with a mobile app is going to be nothing more than a side gig or an infrequent inflow of cash. It is not something that you can substitute for a full-time job or a platform you can rely on for regular income. Nevertheless, you can use the apps to earn an income to pay off debt, supplement income, fund a hobby, and even cover some minor emergencies.

    Legit apps to earn money

    Most money-making applications fall into at least six categories. These include survey, resell your item, accomplish a real-world task, perform an online activity, money-saving, and freelancer applications. If you are looking for legit apps to earn money, here are 25 examples that you should download now for your iOS or Android phone.

    1. BookScouter

    BookScouter helps you sell textbooks and used books for the most money by comparing offers from over 30 book buyback vendors. It is one of the best money making apps that work for everybody.

    The application is perfect for college students that want to sell their old books, individuals looking to declutter their home or office library and make cash, and libraries and book retailers needing to move inventory to free up space.

    2. Capital One Shopping

    Capital One Shopping is one of the best money-saving applications for your iOS or Android device. It helps you save money while you shop online. You can use the app to find deals and savings from your favourite stores, earn rewards and redeem gift cards, and find and apply available coupon codes. Also, you can earn 4% or more in loyalty credits for shopping at local businesses.

    3. Drop

    Drop is an app that offers cashback shopping rewards for all its users. You can earn cash rewards for almost anything you do. That includes taking your morning coffee or offering late-night food deliveries.

    Furthermore, you can earn points for playing games and taking surveys. The app also gives you gift cards for top brands like fashion, beauty, travel, shopping, entertainment, and food brands. With the Drop app, you will get a little something back every time you shop.

    4. Earny

    Earny is one of the best cashback money applications for iOS or Android devices. The app tracks your purchases, deliveries, and travel reservations. It will get you money back if prices of the things you have bought drop or your deliveries arrive late.

    You will get paid to shop with personalised cashback deals, avoid overpaying for items, and get instant refunds, and get back at least $10 per month.

    6. Fiverr

    Fiverr is an iOS and Android application that allows you to make money as a freelancer. It is one of the best online marketplaces for digital freelance services like graphic design, programming, writing, and translation. You will make money for every gig or service you offer.

    The advantage is you can charge as much or as little as you want. However, you will have to pay a small fee to the app for using its platform.

    Foap is an iOS and Android app that allows you to promote and sell your photos. You can use it to turn your passion for photography into dollars. The app also distributes your content through its partners to help you sell even more of your photos and make more money.
