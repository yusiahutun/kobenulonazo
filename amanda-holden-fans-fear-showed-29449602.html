    The Britain's Got Talent judge, 52, posted a set of stunning photos on Instagram, which showed her posing in a gown and throwing her legs in the air in front of a mirror.

    The second snap saw her lying on a bed while wearing a tiny champagne-coloured slip dress, as she promoted the latest range from Revive Beauty.

    While some of her fans expressed their admiration for Amanda's stunning figure, others joked about people "zooming in" to certain areas of the pictures.

    One fan commented that Amanda was "literally the hottest woman on earth," while others echoed the sentiment that she still looked gorgeous despite the revealing poses.

    "How many people have zoomed in to this?" someone quipped, while another Instagram user joked: "We need an Instagram account for her legs alone. Beautiful woman."

    However, Amanda has more on her mind than just lingerie photoshoots, as she prepares to help her 17-year-old daughter, Lexi, launch a modelling career.

    Lexi signed with Storm modelling agency last year, and Amanda has been taking her to relevant events, such as fashion awards shows, to help her prepare for the future.

    Amanda revealed that Lexi is an A-star student, and while she is supportive of her daughter's dreams, she wants her to focus on finishing school before starting her modelling career.

    Geordie presenters Ant and Dec, who have hosted the show since 2007, have also been on tour with the judges.
