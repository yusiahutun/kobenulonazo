    Fatxo's house locked

    TUKO.co.ke reported that DJ Fatxo expressed fear for his life after he was locked out at the gate of his house.

    The house had been sealed off as a crime scene as police investigated the death of Mwathi.

    Mwathi was found dead outside the apartment building after hanging out with DJ Fatxo and his entourage.

    In an interview, DJ Fatxo confessed his fears and worries about his safety.

    He said:

    "I'm tired of all this conflict. I'm a God-fearing person. We have a law, and I think when it gets to this point, somebody should be guided. I don't want all this drama. I feel my life is threatened, and still somebody is keeping me out of my house from 7 pm to midnight. It is not fair to me."

    DJ Fatxo urges DCI to make murder findings public

    On Saturday, May 13, TUKO.co.ke reported that DJ Fatxo requested the DCI disclose information regarding their investigations into the death of Jeff Mwathi.

    He emphasised that the uncertainties surrounding the investigation's outcome have greatly impacted both his personal life and professional music career.

    Following the completion of their inquiry, DCI detectives submitted the report to the Directorate of Public Prosecutions.
