    Kardashian acknowledges "everyone" thinks she's gone under the knife.

    Kim Kardashian has changed quite a bit since everyone first started "Keeping Up with the Kardashians," but she swears it's not due to plastic surgery.

    Though she's fully admitted to Botox and laser hair removal in the past, the reality TV star shot down one rumor about her face that's been following her for years during her her Master Class with Mario Dedivanovic over the weekend.

    "I never had my nose done," she explained at the event. "Everyone thought I did, and I said wait until I have kids because your real features come out."

    As he did her makeup, Mario talked about the small bump on her nose he often hides with contouring, something she even admitted looks different depending on the day. "Pictures, I swear, I look at them and would be like, 'Wow the bump looks so much bigger in some pictures than in others,'" she said during the tutorial.

    The nose talk isn't anything new, as Kim admitted in 2016 that she "used to hate" the bump on it.

    "Hated it," she told Wonderland magazine, "Now I love that it makes me look more ethnic." At the time, she said that it does definitely does "look smaller" than it used to, but suggested that "maybe it's the contour I use."
