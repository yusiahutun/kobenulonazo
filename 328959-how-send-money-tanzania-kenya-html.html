    How to send M-PESA to Tanzania

    M-PESA is a worldwide service that enables its registered customers to receive and send funds globally to several bank accounts and Western Union locations. With PayPal money transfer service, M-PESA customers can access the funds within a short time and shop from any part of the world.

    Having been in the industry for over a decade, M-PESA has significantly reduced the hustle of transferring money to or from your locality. The following are the steps on how to send M-PESA to Tanzania:

    • Click on SIM Toolkit and go to M-PESA
    • Select “Lipa na M-PESA” and click M-PESA Paybill
    • Dial the business number 255255
    • For the account number, enter the mobile number of the Tanzania recipient in the format 2557XXXXXXXX
    • Enter the amount to send in Ksh.
    • Enter your MPESA PIN
    • Confirm the transaction and click OK

    Can I send money to Tanzania from Kenya using my Airtel line?

    As for now, there is no direct option of sending money to Tanzania using Airtel, but you can still transfer the funds to M-PESA then follow the above steps. Here his what you can do to successfully get through.

    • With your Airtel line, dial *222*1#
    • Select the language to use
    • Select send money followed by option 2 that is send to M-PESA
    • Enter the Safaricom number
    • Enter PIN

    Sending money to Tanzania using Chipper Cash

    Chipper Cash is an app that offers quick money transfer across Africa. It allows person to person (P2P) and no-fee payments in selected African countries with plans to reach out to other regions in the near future. Founded in October 2018, Chipper Cash has joined other Fintech startups in the field with the aim of scaling the digital finance platforms across Africa’s population.

    Currently working within East African Countries, the app is destined to enhance economic integration beyond the region. It has also reached Nigeria and Ghana. It is applicable to any mobile money network including Vodacom, M-PESA, Airtel, and MTN.     How can I send money from Tanzania to Kenya?

    Does M-PESA work in Tanzania? Just like you can use Safaricom M-PESA to send money from Kenya to Tanzania, you can also instantly and securely send money from Tanzania to Kenya via Vodacom M-PESA. The following are six vital steps to complete this transaction:

  • Dial *150*00# or use the M-PESA app and choose ‘Send Money”
  • Click on M-PESA Kenya
  • Click send money (check conversion rates before sending)
  • Enter the destination number starting with 2547XXXXXXXX
  • Enter the amount in Tanzanian shillings.
  • Key in your M-PESA PIN and confirm the transaction

    • Based on the above steps, the process of sending money from Tanzania to Kenya is simple, secure and fast. The cost of sending the amount is also affordable since the rates are low compared to other cash transfer methods.
