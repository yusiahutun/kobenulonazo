    If you are someone that loves complete freedom when styling your hair, then investing in a cordless hair dryer will be worth every buck. You might think that a regular corded hair dryer works just fine to dry your hair, but it becomes a styling hassle in many situations. Here are some styling hassles common with regular hair dryers.

    1. Your hair becomes a frizzy mess every time you go for a trek, camp, or road trip.

    2. You don’t find an unoccupied plug in most public restrooms.

    3. Your corded hair dryer takes up too much space in your bag

    4. The cord limits your reach when trying to style your hair.

    5. The plug near you is too loose or prone to minor shocks.

    6. Holding the hair dryer for too long fatigues your arm.

    7. Your current hair dryer wastes too much energy and is harsh on your hair.

    8. You often shower at the gym.

    9. You commonly experience power outages in your space.

    If you relate to any (or all) of these points, then you are in urgent need of a cordless hair dryer. Such heating tools are compact, battery-powered, convenient, and immensely popular among travelers. But what are the features to look for when buying a cordless hair dryer?     How to Choose the Best Battery-powered Hair Dryer?

    The following are the factors to consider before choosing the best battery-powered hair dryer:

    Power Rating: Cordless hair dryers have a default energy-efficient design, as they are not constantly connected to a power source. However, high-wattage dryers (200-400 W) are ideal for styling and a sustainable option over professional corded dryers. 80-100 W is perfect if you want to use a dryer for kids, pets, cleaning, etc.

    Recharge Time: 50 minutes full recharge time makes the cordless hair dryer incredibly efficient. Avoid investing in hair dryers that require overnight recharge if you prioritize energy consumption.

    Size: Lightweight, sleek and travel-friendly designs are ideal for cordless hair dryers. If the holding body of the dryer is too bulky, it will likely cause fatigue in your hand. So, choose dryers that you can conveniently hold.

    Use: Most cordless dryers are easy to use and readily assembled. Look for devices with single switch heat settings, in-built rechargeable batteries, detachable filters, and nozzles for convenient use.

    Dryer Attachments: For various styling purposes, look for devices with detachable concentrators and diffusers. Rotating magnetic nozzles are the cherry on the cake as they allow better angles for airflow.

    Battery: Built-in batteries are the most convenient choice. Ensure the batteries are leak-proof and endure long hours. Opt for rechargeable batteries over disposable ones.

    Price: Good-quality cordless dryers are often expensive as the technology is fairly new. $80 to $170 is a good price range. Nonetheless, make a purchase only if you’re confident about the product’s features.

    If you travel frequently or simply prioritize style at convenience, then cordless hair dryers are a good investment.     FAQ's

    How long do the batteries last in the cordless hair dryers?

    Built-in batteries in good-quality rechargeable cordless hair dryers ideally last for 3-4 hours. Disposable batteries should sustain a good few weeks.

    Are cordless hair dryers necessary for hair care?

    Cordless hair dryers are gentle on the hair follicles and scalp, which prevents damage and provide soft and shiny hair. While not a hair care essential, they are a good investment for styling convenience.
