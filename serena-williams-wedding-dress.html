    Serena Williams Shares Photo of Her Wedding Dress

    Serena Williams has kept tight lipped about her wedding, which took place in New Orleans on Thursday night, but the tennis pro just broke her silence on Instagram to share a very important detail about her nuptials: her dress.

    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Serena Williams has kept tight lipped about her wedding, which took place in New Orleans on Thursday night, but the tennis pro just broke her silence on Instagram to share a very important detail about her nuptials: her dress.

    The newlywed didn't write a caption in her post, but her photo's tags reveal she opted for an Alexander McQueen gown designed by Sarah Burton.

    The frothy white gown featured a voluminous ball skirt and a strapless, sweetheart neckline. Williams kept her hair pulled back in an updo and kept the accessories to a minimum, save a pair of diamond earrings and her massive ring on her left hand.

    It's believed that Williams had at least two dresses on her wedding day. If that's true, this only marks one half of the star athlete's bridal numbers. Considering her loyalty to Versace—from the Met Gala to her first post-baby red carpet appearance—it's possible the Italian designer was responsible for another one of the other looks.

    Williams and her Reddit co-founder beau, Alexis Ohanian, exchanged vows at New Orleans' Contemporary Arts Center among a star-studded crowd. In addition to the couple's closest friends and family, celebrities like Beyoncé, JAY-Z, Kim Kardashian, Eva Longoria, and Ciara were in attendance. The pair's two-month-old daughter, Alexis Olympia Ohanian Jr., was also reportedly present for the occasion.

    The nuptials were Beauty and the Beast-themed, and guests were expected to dress accordingly. While Williams' first dress of the evening doesn't quite scream Belle, it's still fit for a princess.
