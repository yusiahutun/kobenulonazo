    Best All-Around Shirt

    Summersalt The Perfect Boyfriend Shirt

    Best All-Around Shirt

    Summersalt The Perfect Boyfriend Shirt

    Credit: Summersalt

    Summersalt recently expanded from swimwear to everything-wear, but its deep expertise in fabrics make its apparel a must-have. With Summersalt's wrinkle-resistant fabric, this shirt will always look as crisp as the day it was delivered.

    Sizes: XS–2X

    Best Updated Design Shirt

    Alex Mill Double Button Back Shirt in Wide Stripe

    Best Updated Design Shirt

    Alex Mill Double Button Back Shirt in Wide Stripe

    Credit: Alex Mill

    Alex Mill has always cut its women's button-down shirts to men's size gradings—meaning you'll get a perfectly slouchy fit. In this button-down, available in crisp stripes and classic solids, you'll also find buttons along the back vent that open for an even more relaxed fit (and a hint of extra ventilation on the warmest days).

    Early reviewers say this shirt is a must-have. According to one shopper, "Alex Mill really gets the oversized tunic type shirts down perfectly. I'm always looking for a blouse that is unfussy and looks cool the moment you put it on. This is that too. Simile to some of their earlier tunics, it's long and drapes just right."

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Shirt on Amazon

    AYR The Deep End Women's Button Down Shirt

    Best Shirt on Amazon

    AYR The Deep End Women's Button Down Shirt

    Credit: Amazon

    AYR is one of BAZAAR editors' favorite basics brands, and its Deep End button-down is our favorite style in its lineup. It also made Oprah's Favorite Things last year. It's not so oversized as to overwhelm your outfit, but it comes with a lived-in fit and a roomy front pocket. On Amazon, you can find it in several bright shades for layering over a swimsuit or denim shorts. (And for even more detail, we have a detailed AYR shirt review dedicated to how this style lives up to the hype.)

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Short-Sleeve Shirt

    Everlane The Drapey Square Shirt

    Best Short-Sleeve Shirt

    Everlane The Drapey Square Shirt

    Now 14% Off

    Credit: Everlane

    For an oversized button-down that's also vacation-ready, look no further than this short-sleeve shirt. It's made from an eco-conscious Tencel fabric that's gentle on your skin and washes easily—the better for wearing over and over.

    "This is the perfect boxy shirt," one reviewer says. "It drapes nicely and the Tencel is so soft."

    Sizes: XXS–XL

    Best Tested Shirt

    Frankie Shop Melody Oversized Organic Cotton-Poplin Shirt

    Best Tested Shirt

    Frankie Shop Melody Oversized Organic Cotton-Poplin Shirt

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    Sometimes, influencer-beloved items aren't worth the hype. Purchasing Frankie Shop's oversized button-down shirt isn't one of those times. The brand's cotton poplin is lighter than air, while its voluminous cut is made for wearing open over a swimsuit or buttoned up with jeans. Just don't forget the matching boxer shorts.

    This style is also one of the few frequently spotted at our offices: BAZAAR editors own Frankie Shop button-downs in magenta, blue, green, and white.

    Sizes: XS–L

    Best Linen Shirt

    Reformation Will Oversized Linen Shirt

    Best Linen Shirt

    Reformation Will Oversized Linen Shirt

    Credit: Reformation

    Rising temps call for cooling fabrics like linen. On the warmest days, this oversized button-down has the same ease as the others on our list with a fabrication that's extra-breathable.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Patterned Shirt

    Parisienne et alors The Tricolor Lipmann Shirt

    Best Patterned Shirt

    Parisienne et alors The Tricolor Lipmann Shirt

    Credit: Parisienne et alors

    Some of the more adventurous oversized button-downs on the market mix prints and patterns all over the shirt. One of our favorites come from Parisienne et Alors. This pattern is perfect for both a beach-prep or more polished look.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Insider Favorite

    Marie Marot Janet

    Best Insider Favorite

    Marie Marot Janet

    Credit: Marie Marot

    The most in-the-know shoppers go to Marie Marot for their oversized button-downs. "If you're looking for that perfect shirt, I feel like it comes from her," J.Crew women's director Olympia Gayot says of Marie Marot's button-downs. "Her shirts are made out of beautiful British milliner fabric. Every detail on the shirt is perfect. It's based off traditional menswear, so the collar is very crisp and heavy."

    Shop them in solid colors, gingham prints, and stripes of various widths and colors.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Shirt Under $100

    J.Crew Relaxed-Fit Crisp Cotton Poplin Shirt

    Best Shirt Under $100

    J.Crew Relaxed-Fit Crisp Cotton Poplin Shirt

    Credit: J.Crew

    In periwinkle blue, navy stripes, or stark white, J.Crew's oversized button-down is a true classic. It's made from a crisp cotton first used in J.Crew's menswear assortment, and claims the title of the most oversized shirt J.Crew has on offer this year. A custom label and carefully stitched seams make this shirt a polished choice from every angle.

    Reviewers add that the oversized fit holds steady through every size. As one shopper raves,"Girls above a size 18 will be happy with the shirt—it’s extremely oversized [and] feels extremely comfortable and luxurious. I’m so happy and the color is beautiful."

    Sizes: 00–24

    Best Plus-Size Shirt

    Pari Passu Oxford Button-Front Shirt

    Best Plus-Size Shirt

    Pari Passu Oxford Button-Front Shirt

    Credit: Nordstrom

    Oversized button-downs for extended sizes require a few extra considerations, like buttons that won't gape with a large bust. Pari Passu's Oxford shirt achieves this exact effect, in one hundred percent cotton fit to wear year-round.

    Sizes: 14–22

    Best Shirt Under $50

    H&M Oversized Cotton Shirt

    Best Shirt Under $50

    H&M Oversized Cotton Shirt

    Now 39% Off

    Credit: H&M

    A oversized fit so nice, we fully support buying it twice. H&M's cotton poplin shirt comes in seven shades, each with a rounded hem that's slightly longer in the back (the better to tuck into the front of your jeans).

    Reviewers agree this shirt offers great value and endless summer styling opportunities. "This is my favorite blouse that I've purchased from H&M. I don't know why, it's just SO GOOD," one shopper says. "It can be worn so many different ways from office wear to a more grunge look—it's just perfect. The material was not wrinkled when it came out of the box, too so that was a huge plus for me."

    Sizes: XXS–XL

    Best Packable Shirt

    ADAY Something Borrowed Shirt

    Best Packable Shirt

    ADAY Something Borrowed Shirt

    Credit: ADAY

    If you consider a carry-on suitcase your everyday bag, you need ADAY's twist on the oversized button-up shirt. It's made with a technical silk that's machine washable and wrinkle resistant, the better for incorporating into your travel capsule wardrobe kit.

    Reviewers say the versatility in the product description isn't an over-exaggeration. "I travel 5 days a week for work and these are a staple in my wardrobe," one shopper says. "So professional and comfortable. I also love that they don't wrinkle so you can go straight off the plane to a meeting."

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Best Denim Shirt

    Nili Lotan Travis Denim Shirt

    Best Denim Shirt

    Nili Lotan Travis Denim Shirt

    Now 50% Off

    Credit: Net-a-Porter
