    Kourtney Kardashian is laying low and enjoying life as she gets ready to welcome her fourth child.

    The pregnant reality star shared a post on Instagram today from a recent beach and pool day she enjoyed. In the photos, Kardashian is seen posing in a teeny red triangle bikini—a ribbed set with stringy ties on the top and bottom—with her growing baby bump on full display. In the shots, she accessorizes with skinny black rectangle sunglasses and is seen flaunting the vibrant swimwear look in a garden, by the ocean, and in a pool. One clip shows her lounging on a floatie in the pool.

    Kardashian seemed to wear no makeup and had her hair up in a messy topknot.

    “Growing you inside of me, my son, is the greatest blessing, honor and joy,” she wrote in the caption.

    Kardashian is mom to 13-year-old Mason, 10-year-old Penelope and eight-year-old Reign from her former relationship with Scott Disick. She is on her fourth pregnancy, expecting her first child with husband Travis Barker.

    The Poosh founder and Blink-182 drummer, who married in April 2022, announced in June that they were expecting, with a sweet video shared on both of their Instagram accounts. Later in the month, they shared photos from their gender reveal party for their baby and announced they were expecting a boy.

    Throughout her pregnancy, Kardashian has often flaunted her unique maternity style. Last month, she shared an Instagram photo dump featuring photos of four different edgy looks, including a sleek strapless little black dress.
