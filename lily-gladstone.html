    Lily Gladstone (born August 2, 1986) is a American actress. She made her film debut in Jimmy P: Psychotherapy of a Plains Indian (2012), and went on to collaborate with filmmaker Kelly Reichardt in the independent films Certain Women (2016) and First Cow (2019). Her portrayal of a rancher in Certain Women earned her a nomination for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Female.
