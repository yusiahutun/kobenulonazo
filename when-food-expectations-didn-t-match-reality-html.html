    01 Apr 2024, 00:00

    When we've been looking forward to something and our expectations are high, the reality can sometimes disappoint.

    And that's certainly the case for these frustrated foodies, who have shared photos of cooking catastrophes that look nothing like what they were trying to make as well as pre-packaged meals that barely resemble the glossy photos on the packaging.

    From pizza topped with only two measly pieces of pepperoni to some stomach-churning egg dishes that should never have been shared on social media, these are the funniest times when food did not remotely live up to expectations.

    The packaging of this pizza gives you the impression it will be topped with plenty of pepperoni. But when it was opened, there were only two measly pieces of pepperoni on top

    Many of the pictures have been shared on the Reddit group, Expectation Vs Reality, while others have been posted on the image-sharing website Imgur.

    Some are of disastrous cooking attempts, when an amateur chef has tried to replicate a recipe they have coveted with terrible results.

    Others are of disappointing ready meals or sweet treats that don't look even close to how the packaging has advertised them.
