    Add an inch of height, alter a haircut, or slip on a pair of glasses and many of today's biggest country stars look shockingly like other famous celebrities. Blake Shelton, Zac Brown and Toby Keith are just three of the crooning faces that resemble personalities from movies, television, auto racing and... your favorite breakfast.

    Beginning with Brown, check out this list of the 10 country stars and their look-alikes. Along the way, you can decide if the similarities are indeed striking or if it's merely coincidence. Dustin Lynch, Brad Paisley and Jamey Johnson are three others who have well-known doppelgangers. If you can think of another, tell us about it in the comments section below!
