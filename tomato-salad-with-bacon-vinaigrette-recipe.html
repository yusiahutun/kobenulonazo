    Best Tomato Salad With Bacon Vinaigrette

    This bright and beautiful salad is filled with flavor, thanks to fresh heirloom tomatoes and a homemade bacon vinaigrette.

    Read More

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Yields: 8 serving(s)

    Total Time: 15 mins

    Ingredients

    Save Recipe
    • 4

      slices thick-cut bacon, chopped

    • 1

      small onion, chopped 

    • 3 tbsp.

       sherry vinegar

    • 2 tsp.

      Dijon mustard

    • 2 tbsp.

      olive oil

    • 1 lb.

      Heirloom tomatoes, cut into wedges

    • 1 oz.

      blue cheese, crumbled

    • Fresh chives, chopped

    Directions

    • Step 1Cook bacon in a medium skillet on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until crisp, 8 to 10 mins. Transfer to a paper-towel-lined plate with a slotted spoon; pour off all but 2 Tbsp. grease. Add onion. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 4 to 6 minutes. Add sherry vinegar and Dijon mustard; cook, scraping up any brown bits, until incorporated, 1 minute. Remove from heat; whisk in olive oil. Arrange tomatoes on a platter and drizzle with vinaigrette. Top with bacon, blue cheese, and chives.
