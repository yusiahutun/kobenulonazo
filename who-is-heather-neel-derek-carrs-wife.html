    Derek Carr is a famous NFL star whose career in the NFL kicked off in 2014 when the Oakland Raiders drafted him. He was the 36th overall pick. Following his entry into the NFL, Carr’s career quickly progressed. It did not take long for him to win over the hearts of his team’s passionate fanbase. 

    By 2022, Carr landed a $125.5 million three-year contract thanks to his skills and execution. He is currently the quarterback for the Las Vegas Raiders. When he is not on the field, Carr, a doting husband, can be found at home with his wife, Heather Neel, and their kids.

    Keep reading to learn more about her. 

    Who Is Heather Neel? 

    Heather Neel is famously known as Derek Carr’s wife. However, she also has a career of her own. The gorgeous woman was born in Northern California on January 3, 1990. She attended Fresno Christian High School and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree from Western Colorado University.

    Neel is very socially oriented and often posts cute pictures of herself and her husband on and off the field. She married Carr in 2012, so she is best known as Carr’s wife among sporting fans. Outside sporting circles, Neel is a social media influencer with a considerable following on Instagram, where she shares adorable posts.

    Heather Neel and Derek Carr

    Carr and Neel’s romance goes way back to when they were just college students at Fresno State. However, the couple did not meet until he entered her workplace at BJ’s Brewhouse during his sophomore year. Neel was working one of her shifts at the time, and Carr had entered as a customer and engaged her in a conversation.

    Neel recalled the encounter in an old interview: “I had like diamond earrings in my ear, and I had them both. I knew I did. And he said, ‘Why are you only wearing one earring?’ And it made me check my ears. And I was like, ‘That’s your pickup line?’ [It worked] it did work. I talked to him.”

    After that first meeting, Neel and Carr started dating, and soon after, they married. It was a great decision for Carr because, after the wedding, he went on to have two of his best seasons, scoring 87 touchdown passes against 15 interceptions for the Bulldogs, helping them set a 20-6 record.

    Those two seasons put Carr on the radar and eventually got him drafted by the Raiders in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft.

    Heather Neel Kids 

    Neel and her husband Carr have welcomed four children in their years together. Their children include Dallas, Deker, Deakon, and Brooklyn.

    Unfortunately, parenthood started with a crisis for Carr and Neel as their son Dallas had to undergo three emergency surgeries mere days after his birth. 

    Carr once rehashed the experience: “I’ve been around nieces and nephews, and I’ve seen them throw up. But he started throwing up this dark green,” he said. “He was laying in his little bassinet, and it was shooting out to the floor.”

    Twelve hours later, Dallas received emergency surgery to correct his tangled intestines at Children’s Hospital Central California. He returned for another surgery the second time he visited the hospital and did so again two weeks later. 

    It worked, and Dallas has been able to live a happy and healthy life. 

    Heather Neel Career

    Before meeting Carr, Heather Neel held down a job as a waitress at a steakhouse. She has since morphed into a social media influencer and model. Besides her job as an influencer, Neel manages the merchandise website DC4merchandise.com.

    Heather Neel’s Net Worth

    Neel’s net worth is currently unknown. However, she enjoys a luxurious lifestyle thanks to her husband’s net worth. The NFL star is said to be worth a whopping $85 million.

    Heather Neel Age

    Neel was born on January 3, 1990, which makes her 33 years old in January 2023. 

    Heather Neel Instagram

    Neel’s Instagram username is @heathercarr4. At the time of this publication, she has more than 50,000 followers and follows around 350 people.

    She is fond of sharing posts of her family, and her fans love her for it. Her posts show that even though she and the NFL star have been married for years, they still deeply love one another.

    Carr is still an active player in the NFL but will soon be hanging up his boots permanently. Many wonder what will come next for him; will he go into entertainment like Terry Crews? Or will he remain in the sporting world? 

    Only time will tell.
