    Rihanna Just Wore the $38 Hatch Bra Expecting Moms Love

    Outfit by outfit, statement belly chain by skin-tight, feather-hemmed mini dress, Rihanna is redefining maternity style. Even her more comfort-driven looks are outfit inspiration for expecting moms, including one featuring a $38, fan-favorite maternity bra.

    27 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Outfit by outfit, statement belly chain by skin-tight, feather-hemmed mini dress, Rihanna is redefining maternity style. Even her more comfort-driven looks are outfit inspiration for expecting moms, including one featuring a $38, fan-favorite maternity bra.

    Last week, Rihanna wore an all-black outfit including rolled-down overalls and a scoop-neck black bra, overlaid with gold jewelry. Her laid-back bra was quickly identified as the "Skin to Skin" bra by the maternity and postpartum label Hatch. For now, it's still available in several sizes.

    Hatch The Skin to Skin Bra

    Hatch The Skin to Skin Bra

    Credit: Hatch

    If Rihanna wasn't going to wear a bra from her own lingerie line, it was going to be from Hatch. The brand regularly appears in celebrity maternity wardrobes, including Meghan Markle, Kate Hudson, and Olivia Wilde. Rihanna's specific bra has also been spotted on model Ashley Graham.

    What makes the "Skin to Skin" bra worthy of Rihanna's maternity wardrobe is a blend of technical design features and easygoing comfort. It was initially designed as part of the label's "Fourth Trimester" collection, filled with nursing- and postpartum-friendly pieces like fold-down nursing tanks and hardwire-free bras. While this bra's elastic top is ideal for nursing, the ultra-soft fabric can also adjust and stretch with you during pregnancy.

    A rep for Hatch confirmed to BAZAAR.com that the "Skin to Skin" bra is now one of the brand's best-selling styles across the entire store, thanks to its near-universal appeal. Moms-to-be often give the bra a 5/5 star rating. "So far the most comfortable bra I have worn during pregnancy when everything feels too tight and binding," one reviewer says. "The shape is pretty natural, which I am all about these days." Another writes that they purchased it in every color available, because "the fabric is soft and stretchy for my growing boobs, but supportive at the same time."

    Reviewers who've already reached the fourth trimester say it's the most comfortable bra to wear while breastfeeding. "These have been my favorite bras for nursing! They’re insanely comfortable and I frequently sleep in them as well," another enthusiastic reviewer says.

    Pick up Rihanna's Hatch bra and other essentials from the celebrity-beloved label below. New prints in the "Skin to Skin" bra are due later in April.
