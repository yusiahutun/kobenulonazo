    Just in time for the highly anticipated Season 2 premiere of Bridgerton, Pat McGrath Labs has released its Bridgerton II collection, with series star Nicola Coughlan serving as the muse. Featuring a new MTHRSHP eye shadow palette, blush set, body shimmer, kohl eyeliner, and range of satiny lipsticks, the line channels Regency-era beauty with a slightly modern twist.

    "Pat McGrath has pushed the luxe modernity of Bridgerton beyond the screen with this collaboration, and we are excited to share it with the world," Shondaland's chief design and digital media officer, Sandie Bailey, said of the collaboration in a press release. "She is an icon and a global force in the beauty and fashion industry, and shares a creative passion for projects that evoke emotion, making her a perfect partner as we expand the Bridgerton world."

    Shop the Pat McGrath Labs x Bridgerton II Collection

    Pat McGrath Labs products were exclusively used on the show's cast (which included up to 150 actors a day) while filming its latest season, and they were applied by BAFTA Award-winning hair and makeup designer Erika Ökvist. "My first love affair with Pat McGrath Labs products started when I saw the range of foundations—they're fluid and invisible," Ökvist says. "I can honestly say the products set beautifully on everybody's skin, and there was a color and shade to fit every character, scene, and moment on set. Every actor in the show wanted to go home with their makeup on; everyone felt radiant and beautiful."

    While on set, the designer used the collection's softer and more muted MTHRSHP: Belle of the Ball eye shadow palette to create Bridgerton's natural-looking, daytime Regency looks, and its bolder, more glimmery shades for the show's nighttime ballroom scenes.

    As for the line's inspiration, Lady Whistledown herself, Coughlan says Pat McGrath Labs' ethos aligns perfectly with her beauty interests and counts the SatinAllure Lipstick shade Négligée as her new perfect nude. "I'm a makeup girl, so it was like a dream going to Pat's studio," she says. "The Belle of the Ball palette is so gorgeous. It's so pigmented, it's so sparkly. I love the ethos of Pat McGrath Labs. I adore the foundation, the way you can layer the products—it just looks so much more fresh and real."

    Aside from the pigmented formulas and versatile colors Pat McGrath Labs products are known for, the second Bridgerton range also features powder-pink packaging with a quilted pattern and cameo design that nods to the upper-crust families of the 1800s. The collection starts at $28 and is available to shop by individual product or through a variety of sets that ladder up to $410 for the entire collection.
