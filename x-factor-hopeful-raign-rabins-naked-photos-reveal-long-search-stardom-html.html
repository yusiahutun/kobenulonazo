    Raign immediately got off to a bad start with Cheryl when she walked into the audition room and Cheryl asked: 'Why do you think music hasn't worked out for you so far?'

    Offended by the question, the 31-year-old hopeful replied: 'It is working out for me! I have a song that is number 17 in the charts in Russia at the moment.'

    Cheryl then gently pressed her on the matter, asking why she wasn't signed to a record label.

    After performing Zed Clarity, Raign was given a yes by Mel and Louis. However, Simon was unimpressed and said no after telling her she didn't sing the song well.

    Distraught, Raign then profusely apologised to Simon.

    Second chances: The hopeful refuses to let the opportunity slips through her fingers and heads back to the audition room to ask if she can perform again

    With that, Cheryl was left irritated and told her no. 'My answer has a lot to do with what you just said in the room. Honestly,’ she explained.
