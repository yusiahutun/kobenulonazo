    The Duchess of Cambridge wears the same dress as Paris Hilton

    Getty The Duchess of Cambridge and Kendall Jenner are two of the world's most stylish women. What else do they have in common? This week, both wore looks that appear to have been influenced by Paris Hilton.

    11 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Getty

    The Duchess of Cambridge and Kendall Jenner are two of the world's most stylish women. What else do they have in common? This week, both wore looks that appear to have been influenced by Paris Hilton.

    Last night, the Duchess of Cambridge attended the premiere of A Street Cat Named Bob in aid of Action on Addiction. She wore a beautiful white dress by Self-Portrait with a thigh-high split and partly sheer section. The dress was identical to the gown Hilton was seen in at the amfAR Gala in Los Angeles last week.

    Getty

    And the Duchess was not the only A-lister spotted in an outfit inspired by Hilton. Jenner attended her 21st-birthday party in Los Angeles on Wednesday night wearing a very revealing silver dress that was almost exactly the same as the one Hilton wore to her 21st-birthday party in 2002.

    Getty

    Jenner later posted a picture of herself in the dress on Instagram and said, "Vintage Paris Hilton vibes". While the model's choice may have been intentional, it's likely that the Duchess wasn't aware of who had sported the dress before her, but we're sure it wouldn't have altered her decision to wear the beautiful gown.
