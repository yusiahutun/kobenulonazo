    These are the key pieces in the Duchess of Sussex's wardrobe...

      The Duchess of Sussex has described herself as a classic California girl in the past, and we think summer is her season.

      With that in mind, Meghan has hot weather dressing nailed - and be it floaty dresses, summer sandals or chic sunglasses, she most definitely has some style staples that she always returns to.

      Plus, being a busy mum to son Archie Harrison and daughter Lilibet Diana we bet Meghan is embracing comfort and ease more than ever! Here are seven of her tried-and-tested summer style staples...

      • A Sleeveless Trench Dress

        If there's anything Meghan loves as much as she loves a neutral palette, it's a great tailored sleeveless dress in a blazer or trench style.

      • Get The Look: H&M Trench Dress

        We think Meghan would love H&M's trench dress, which is fitted, falls just above the knee and features a tie belt as well as subtle shoulder pads.

        £39.99 at H&M

      • A Crisp Button Down Shirt

        Meghan has long loved a classic shirt for both casual or formal occasions. She usually teams them with jeans while off-duty, and is partial to a striped piece.

      • Get The Look: Boden Oversized Striped Shirt

        The Duchess chose a blue and white striped Ralph Lauren shirt for Wimbledon in 2018, and this one from Boden is a great lookalike. 

        £37.50 at Boden

      • A Breezy Midi Dress

        In the hot weather, Meghan is often seen wearing something loose-fitting and relaxed, particularly since becoming a mother. The Duchess was pictured in a comfy-looking linen dress as she enjoyed a family day out at the polo with Archie, Kate and the Cambridge kids in July 2019. She was also spotted in a gingham shirt dress at another polo appearance in June 2018, and a popular Reformation number while on tour in Australia.

      • Get The Look: Reformation Tace Dress

        We could definitely see Meghan in Reformation's Tace midi dress. Made from linen, it features an open back and a breezy A-line skirt.

        £248 at Reformation

      • A Versatile Black Jumpsuit

        Meghan's key wardrobe looks include her simple black jumpsuit by Everlane, which she has dressed both up and down, depending on the occasion.

      • Get The Look: Nobody's Child Halter Neck Jumpsuit

        We've found a very similar look in a comfy linen-blend at Nobody's Child.

        £75 at Nobody's Child

      • A White Blazer

        Meghan can't resist a great tailored white blazer, whether worn as part of a suit - as she did recently, in Valentino - or as a single piece over a dress or pair of jeans.

      • Get The Look: Mango Suit Blazer

        Thankfully these days you can find well-made blazers that won't break the bank, like this gorgeous high street piece from Mango.

        £59.99 at Mango

      • On-Trend Sunglasses

        The Duchess has an extensive sunglasses collection, from round frames to aviators! She often opts for more modern frames than the styles we tend to see on the Duchess of Cambridge - like her Le Specs Air Heart frames.

      • Get The Look: Le Specs Air Heart Sunglasses

        Despite being a favourite of Meghan's, her Le Specs sunglasses are very affordable. You can shop the bestselling frames for £55 at Net-a-Porter.

        £55 at Net-a-Porter

      • Boho Sandals

        Meghan's favourite strappy sandals are hands down her gladiator-style Sarah Flint 'Grear' flats, which have been a staple of hers since before she met Prince Harry. She teams them with everything from jeans to dresses and shorts, and even took them along with her for her first overseas tour in November 2018.

      • Get The Look: Free People Vacation Day Sandals

        Get the look with these boho strappy leather sandals from Free People. They feature wraparound ankle detail with a lace-up back.

        £74 at Free People
