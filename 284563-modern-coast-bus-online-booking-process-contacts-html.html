    Easy steps for Modern Coast bus online booking

    Below are steps you can follow to do your online booking.

  • Visit their official Modern Coast online booking portal.
  • Select the departure point, destination location, and travel date to book a ticket. After that, click "Search available buses."
  • You will be taken to a new page to view all available buses, time schedules, and prices.
  • Select the bus of your choice depending on the departure time indicated on the page. Prices for available seats include VIP, first-class, and business class. You must make a decision based on your preferences or budget.
  • The following step is to choose your seat number. You will also choose your boarding and dropping points and click continue.
  • A page will appear, and you can sign up with your email address, sign in, or continue as a guest. Choose any that you prefer.
  • Following that, you must enter your passenger information. This information includes your name, identification or passport number, phone number, and email address. After that, click "proceed to review trip."
  • You can make payments after you have reviewed your information. MPESA is the preferred method of payment. The official paybill number is 525600.
  • After you make your payment, you will receive an SMS from MPESA and a confirmation text from Modern Coast. Your ticket number is included in the message.

    Routes covered by Modern Coast bus

    Since 2007 when Modern Coast bus services began, the company has grown exponentially. The buses operate in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda.

    It is worth noting that buses have power outlets, an internet connection, and a sleeping couch. The company has 30 branches in Kenya in various towns such as Migori, Kisii, Nakuru, Mombasa, Nairobi, Meru, Embu, Busia, Kericho, and Kisumu.
