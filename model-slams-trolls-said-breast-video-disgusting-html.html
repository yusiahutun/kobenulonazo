    A model has hit back at trolls who criticised her for uploading a video of her nephew, two, touching her breasts.

    Tillie Medland, 20, from Toronto, shared the video - which shows her nephew Jetson putting his hand down her sports bra - with her 19,000 Instagram followers.

    People have slammed the video, which has had 65,000 views, 'disgusting' - and some even suggested that Jetson's parents should go to prison over the footage. 

    Tillie, however, believes that people are taking the footage too seriously, and says it is 'hilarious and heartwarming'.

    People took to the comments section in their droves to air their views on the footage, which was seemingly filmed by Jetson's mother.

    Commenting on the video, which sees a shocked and bemused Tillie laughing as her nephew places his hand down her top, one user wrote: 'Sick f*** she is. Allowing a child to touch her like that. Had she been a man the whole world would be labeling him a pedophile and a pig for letting a child touch him. What a shame.'
