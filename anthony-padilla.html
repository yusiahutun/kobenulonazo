    Daniel Anthony Padilla ( pə-DEE-yə; born September 16, 1987) is an American YouTuber, interviewer and actor. He gained prominence for co-founding the YouTube channel Smosh with Ian Hecox, where he starred in sketch comedy videos with Hecox from 2005 to 2017, and announced his return to the channel in 2023. Outside of Smosh, Padilla hosts a series of interviews titled I Spent a Day With..., where he interviews fellow Internet celebrities, as well as survivors of crime and natural disasters.
