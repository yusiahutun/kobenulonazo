AEG Live Acquires Starland Ballroom

01 Jun 2024, 00:00

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess Media Wire) — AEG Live has acquired ownership of New Jersey’s the Starland Ballroom as part of the company’s overall strategic growth in the greater New York and New Jersey metro area. Located in Sayreville, NJ, the Starland Ballroom joins AEG Live’s expanding roster of recently acquired venues in New Jersey such as Red Bull Park, a 25,000-seat stadium currently under construction in Harrison, which will become the home of Major League Soccer’s New York Red Bulls as well as national concert tours promoted by AEG Live; Newark's Prudential Center, which will be the new home of the National Hockey League’s New Jersey Devils upon the arena’s forthcoming opening; and the Poland Spring Arena at The Ritacco Center in Tom’s River, which will feature co-exclusive booking of events with the venue’s former owners Concerts East, which will maintain partnership with AEG Live.
