    The presenter is a proud mum-of-one

      We love settling down to watch BBC's Escape to the Country on weekdays. The show has been running since 2002, so it's clearly popular among its viewers, and the presenters, such as Nicki Chapman and Jonnie Irwin, have also become household names thank to their stints on the show. One star of the BBC programme is Ginny Buckley.

      MORE: All you need to know about Escape to the Country star Ginny Buckley

      The TV star has had a hugely successful career working in journalism, broadcasting and radio. But when she isn't busy working in the gorgeous countryside, Ginny can be found at home with her family. Meet her family below…

      WATCH: Take a look inside fellow presenter Nicki Chapman's glorious garden 

      Does Ginny Buckley have children?

      Yes! The BBC star is a proud mum-of-one to son Zak, aged 12. Ginny clearly loves taking her son on adventures across the globe as the pair spent this year's Easter holidays enjoying the sun in Australia. 

      More recently, the pair journeyed down to Cornwall to go cliff jumping in Port Gaverne. 

      The presenter marked the special moment by sharing a picture of the pair to Instagram. She wrote the caption: "Thank you to the wonderful @cornishadventure for achieving the impossible and giving me the confidence to climb up a cliff and jump off it into the brilliant blue ocean!

      "I'm just disappointed we didn't spot one of those #seabadgers! I didn't quite manage to get to the heights the boy did, but I'm proud of how far I pushed myself."

      Ginny Buckley with her son, Zak, in Australia

      What has Ginny Buckley said about her family?

      Ginny celebrated her son's recent 12th birthday by sharing a gorgeous photo of the mum and son on Instagram back in August last year. 

      The 52-year-old wrote: "And just like that my beautiful little boy is 12! Happy Birthday to my funny, kind and caring August boy, who is at his happiest down by the beach catching a wave! 

      "Built that ladder to the stars Zaka… I'm so proud to be your mum and I love you very much."

      Ginny posted this sweet snap back in August

      Is Ginny Buckley married?

      The presenter is clearly protective of her private life, and there's no sign of a partner on her social media. Therefore, it's not known whether the presenter is married or in a relationship.
