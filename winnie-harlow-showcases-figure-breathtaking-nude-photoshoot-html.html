    Winnie Harlow looked breathtaking as she posed for a stunning nude photoshoot for the latest issue of Women's Health UK.

    The America's Next Top Model star, 28, showcased her gym-honed physique in the photoshoot and detailed how she maintains her supermodel body. 

    Winnie kicked back in the great outdoors for the shoot, posing on a set of white steps in the desert. 

    The model sported a pair of white-rimmed sunglasses and matching trainers, while she let her locks fall loose down her shoulders.

    Winnie detailed her fitness regime, citing squats and Russian twists as two of her favourite exercises.

    Winnie, who has the skin condition vitiligo, also revealed how she faced plenty of rejection in the early days of her career.

    She said: 'My younger self always believed in the power of faith, strength and perseverance, while learning vulnerability and being authentically myself. I got a lot of 'no's along the way, but I just pushed through.'

    Winnie, whose real name is Chantelle Brown-Young, also discussed where her stage name came from.

    She said: 'Back then, everyone had a nickname. If your name was Chanel, you'd be, like, Chan-Chan Monroe, or whatever. Bubblez with a Z, all that type of stuff. 

    'I loved Winnie the Pooh when I was growing up and I was also obsessed with Marilyn Monroe, and her inspiration was Jean Harlow.'

    Adding on the empowerment this gives, she shared: 'It’s beautiful that I can help inspire confidence in people, no matter what walk of life they are from'.
