    From the moment Zayden Banks came into the world, it was inevitable that people would be curious about him. This curiosity is based on being the son of the famous rapper Lil Durk.

    Lil Durk is a household name in the entertainment industry. The famous American rapper has been active in the hip-hop world since 2010. He ultimately rose to prominence in 2020 and has since gained the attention of millions of fans worldwide.

    Born Durk Derrick Banks on October 19, 1992, the American rapper rose to stardom after his first Billboard Hot 100 entry with his single, Viral Moments, off his fifth studio album. His features with other famous rappers like Lil Baby and Drake propelled him to his first Billboard Top Ten entry and gained him a cult following of fans.

    However, fame and privacy do not go hand in hand, as being a famous rapper has a way of putting your family and kids in the spotlight. Zayden Banks, although a minor, gained the attention of his father’s fans. Fans are curious to know everything there is to know about Lil Durk’s mini-me, from the identity of his mother to his siblings and his age.

    Here’s everything we know about the celebrity kid.

    Who Is Zayden Banks?

    Zayden Banks is the third child and second son of American rapper Lil Durk. Like many celebrity kids, he is famous because of his father’s celebrity status. Born in late 2013, Zayden is an African American. 

    Unlike his father, Zayden Banks enjoys the presence of his paternal grandfather, Dontay Banks. Dontay Banks, popularly known as Big Durk, has been a part of his grandson’s childhood and upbringing. Unfortunately, Dontay couldn’t do the same for his son, Lil Durk. 

    According to reports, Dontay Banks was arrested for drug-related crimes when Lil Durk was barely a year old and was in and out of jail for most of his son’s life. As a result of his incarceration, Dontay Banks missed out on most of his son’s childhood. However, he is making up for it by actively being a part of his grandson’s upbringing.

However, given his age, Zayden Banks has much growing up to do, and we cannot predict if he will follow in his father’s footsteps or forge a different path.

Although Zayden’s father has been romantically involved with several women since he rose to fame, he has managed to keep most of their identities from the public. As far as Lil Durk’s relationship goes, his first relationship was with Nicole Covone, the mother of his first two kids.

    The pair sparked dating rumors in 2008 and separated years later when he started dating Tameka Kute. The famous rapper is engaged to his longtime sweetheart, India Royale. However, none of these women is Zayden Banks’ mom. 

    Looking at his birth year, we can confirm that Lil Durk was with Zayden Banks’ mom around the same time he was with Nicole Covone. Whoever Zayden Banks’ mother is, she is one of the romantic partners the famous rapper has cloaked in secrecy.
