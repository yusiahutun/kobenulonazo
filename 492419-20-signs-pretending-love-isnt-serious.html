    Relationships can be complex. It is often said that in most relationships, one person loves more than the other. This may not be scientifically true, but some people can attest to the experience. While it may not be easy to tell who is doing more loving, one can look out for tell-tale signs. But which are the top signs she is pretending to love you but isn't serious?

    How do you tell if a girl is faking a relationship? Apart from the signs, you can also try to converse with her and listen to how she responds. Her answers on profound relationship discussions and your future together will tell you everything you need to know.

    If you are sharp, you will get her stand in the first few conversations. When doing this, remember not to make it obvious, and do not be too direct. Do not turn the conversation into an interrogation, as you will not achieve what you had planned. Instead, talk to her warmly, and be as engaging as possible.

    Signs she is pretending to love you

    If you are in a relationship with a woman you love but are unsure of how she feels about the relationship, you can look for signs that she is pretending to love you. The signs may not be evident at first, but with time you will learn about her and how she behaves around you. Here are the top 20 sure signs that she is pretending to love:

    1. She is distant

    How do you tell if a girl is faking a relationship? She is barely present when you need her company. The girl will avoid you whether you are on speaking terms or bad terms. She will not check up on you, and when you try to talk to her, she will respond just to be nice to you.

    She can go a week without talking to you if you do not reach out first. This kind of behaviour should be your first red flag, as someone distant most likely has zero interest in you or what you have to offer.

    2. She dismisses talks about the future

    You will try to bring up talks about your future together, your dreams, your plans, and even family life, but she will be least interested. This is because she does not see a future with you.

    As a man, you are only there to fulfil her current desires, whatever they may be, but not plan a life in the future. If you notice that conversations about the future are only coming from you, then you already have your answer about the state of your relationship.

    3. You never know what is really going on with her

    How do you tell if a girl is just keeping you around? You can never tell if she is having a difficult time at work or in a terrible mood. You cannot read what is going on in her head because everything about her is vague.

    4. Suggests a break up after every fight

    Quarrels, fights, and arguments are inevitable in every relationship. Subtle arguments are, in fact, a good sign in a relationship as it shows that both parties are free to express themselves and learn from each other. However, too many arguments every other day should be a concern.

    If you happen to be in a relationship where your girl is constantly throwing the break-up word after every minor argument, then know that she wants out of the relationship and is looking for a tiny excuse to be on her way.

    5. She never texts first

    There are no rules to texting when in a relationship. Anyone can text first. Do you feel like talking? A simple "Hey, love" will do you good. Take a moment and think about the number of times you have texted first and compare it to the number of times she hits you up first.

    6. Her ex features in her conversations one too many times

    The past should remain in the past. No one wants to hear about their lover's ex, especially when they are conversing. A lady that keeps mentioning her ex is one to stay away from. She is most likely keeping you around because the ex wants nothing to do with her.

    She is still hung up on him and will run back to his arms if a chance presents itself. It is one of the major signs a girl is using you for attention. She compares you to her ex because she probably misses him or just compares him to you to mess with your ego.

    7. She has never offered to pay for anything

    It is the norm for men to pay when a couple is going out or getting something for themselves. However, a girl in love will never hesitate to chip in every once in a while. You cannot be the only one who pays for dates, especially if she is the one who came up with the idea of a date.

    She has never paid for a take-out, bought you your favourite smoothie, or even forgot to get you something nice for your birthday. She simply does not care about you.

    8. She owns a bag full of excuses

    She is always full of excuses. You will ask her why she has not done something you had asked her to, and she will bring up a long list of excuses. You want to hang out with her at her place, and she will, out of the blue, remember that she has errands to run.

    10. She is flirty with other guys

    This is one of the major red flags in anyone. A person who is flirty with everyone does not have any romantic feelings towards you. You will confront her about it, but she will continue with the habit because she wants you to get livid and leave her.

    11. You have never met her friends or family

    Do you know any of her friends? What about her family? No one in her circle knows of you. You have probably shown her to your friends and even made her meet your brother or sister, but you don't know anything about her.

    The red flag is a major sign that she is not serious about the relationship. If you mattered to her, she could have invited you to meet her friends, taken you to her favourite hanging out spots, and maybe had you chat with her mom on the phone.

    She hates it when you ask her to make it official, as you are ruining her plans. In her life, you are hidden like something illegal. Have some dignity and exit the relationship.

    13. She is critical of you

    She says she loves you, but her actions say otherwise. You will make one wrong move or make a mistake, which is the only thing she will discuss. She is extremely critical of you, more critical than she is of other people.

    You cannot breathe around her because you are scared of making mistakes. She will lament about a mistake you made weeks ago and remind you every moment of how much of a mess you made. Being in the same environment with that individual is not good for your mental health.

    14. She rarely compliments you

    Women love receiving compliments and will reciprocate if they love what they see. When was the last time your girlfriend told you that you looked good? You went out of your way to change your wardrobe but did not receive a single compliment from her.

    Why? She does not love you the way you imagine she does. She almost certainly just tolerates you because she is still looking for the ideal man for herself.

    15. Too secretive

    A secretive person is one to stay away from. Why too many secrets if you are leading a straight life? She has loads of secrets from her past that affect your relationship's well-being. She does not open up on anything but deals with issues in the dark.

    This is one of the biggest signs that she is not in love with you. Being with a girl with too many secrets may get you in trouble, so you better watch out if your girlfriend is of this kind.

    16. Everything is on her terms

    The movie you are to watch in the evening, the location for your date, the meal you are to eat, and practically anything that concerns the both of you are on her terms. You let her have it her way only because you love her.

    She does not care that you want a change of scenery or have different plans for the day, she will demand what pleases her, and you will, unfortunately, give her. You need to love yourself a little more and not let people have too much control over your life.

    17. She is unpredictable

    An unpredictable person is such a buzz kill. She will get you in high spirits but will kill the mood. With her, you can get hot and cold vibes at any time. Yet, she can be happy in the morning and will be a different person when the sun sets.

    What could be the reason for her mood swings? You will never know because she is erratic, never confides in you, and barely lets you in her world.

    18. She will holla only if she needs something

    She never calls first unless she needs something. She will only remember your number when she needs a favour from you. It does not matter how big or small the favour is; she merely runs to you when she is in need.

    Like the good boyfriend you are, you will sort out her needs, and that will be her last call to you until she needs something else. One thing you should take from this kind of partner is that you are being used.

    19. You do not exist on her socials

    Everyone uses their social media differently. Some users make their social media pages their diaries, while others share little. This may not be a real sign.

    If you knew how she moved on social media before you were an item, things might be more precise. If she always talks about her previous lovers but does not mention anything about your relationship online, then there is a need to worry.

    20. You are just her fallback plan

    You asked her out, and she probably said yes out of boredom or because she could not get the guy she was targeting. Your little meet-ups turned into a full-blown relationship, but she is jaded again and only uses you as she waits for her perfect man.

    She loves your company but only when she is lonely or bored. You are the last thing on her priority list, and she only has time for you when she does not have anything meaningful to do.

    Relationships sometimes need a little work and intelligence to prosper. Both parties need to be in love and not have a wandering eye. If your lover is the one who pays zero attention to you, then you should be wary of the signs she is pretending to love you. If you notice any sign, try to communicate with her, and if there is no change from her end, then it is time to call off the relationship.
