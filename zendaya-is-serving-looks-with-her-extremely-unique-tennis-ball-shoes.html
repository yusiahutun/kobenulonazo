    12 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Just when we thought Zendaya couldn’t top her last look, she’s done it again, smashing the tennis fashion theme with her shoes as she promotes her upcoming film, Challengers.

    Zendaya and her celebrity stylist Law Roach never miss with her fashion, and lately, she’s been acing her tennis looks. Earlier this week, she was joined by a giant croissant in Paris, and now she’s headed to Rome with tennis balls stuck to her heels, and we are obsessed.

    Zendaya stuns in unique tennis ball shoes

    Taking to Rome to continue her promotion of the new movie, which sees her character caught in the middle of a love triangle between two tennis players, the 27-year-old stunned in a plunging silver dress with a tennis-inspired pleated skirt.

    The star pinned her blonde locks back in the sparkly number, but it was her shoes that were getting all the attention.

    The white stilettos had tennis balls engraved onto the heels, and fans were obsessed with the look.

    As the look went viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, fans did not hesitate to comment. “Oh I’m living for the tennis ball heels,” wrote one impressed user.

    “She served!” exclaimed another. (Pardon the pun.)

    A third wrote: “Zendaya and Margot Robbie have the movie press tour fashion game on.”

    “She never loses when it comes to fashion. Her style is impeccable,” complimented another.

    The star is co-charing the Met Gala

    Who better to co-chair the “Oscars of fashion” than the Euphoria star herself? This year’s theme will be Sleeping Beauties: Reawakening Fashion, and joining Zendaya on the hosting committee will be Bad Bunny, Chris Hemsworth, and Jennifer Lopez.

    The Hollywood star, who is currently dating Tom Holland, has graced the steps five times and has produced some highly memorable outfits including her 2017 flowery Dolce & Gabbana gown.

    After her premiere looks this year, we can’t wait to see what she pulls out of the bag!
