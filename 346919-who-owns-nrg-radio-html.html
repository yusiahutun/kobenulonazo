    If you are looking for a radio station that combines both music and entertainment, NRG Radio should be on top of your list. The station delivers all types of songs such as riddims, gospels, reggae, and other forms of entertainment. But, do you know who owns NRG Radio?

    NRG Radio is a radio station that broadcasts from Nairobi, Kenya. This station went on air on 1st March 2018. It was launched to change how people listen to their music since it made its radio accessible through the internet. It has several scheduled programs, including NRG Transit, the AM Show, and the NRG Breakfast club.

    What does NRG radio stand for?

    So, have you been asking what does NRG radio stands for? Well, it stands for energy, which gives young people the morale of watching and streaming music and movies online. NRG radio does not only present entertainment, but it also provides the best updates and the latest news from Kenya and other countries. This station provides information to all Kenyans regardless of whether they are literate or illiterate.

    NRG Radio owner

    Who owns NRG Radio? Who is Kelvin Mulei? These are some of the questions that pop up when the issue of NRG ownership is discussed. Well, Mulei is the owner of the NRG radio station. Kelvin is also a businessman and a founder of the popular Groove Awards. He is also the CEO of Mo Sound Events. Mulei was once a DJ, and his main vision was to use the music platform and reward, promote and celebrate the young talented youths who used to spread the Lord’s message in their songs.

    Mo Sound Events, which is managed by Kelvin, is known to be a creative company that provides elegant solutions, including production, events management, digital media, and entertainment equipment.

    NRG Radio launch

    NRG Radio was launched in March 2018. Before this radio station was launched, it was preceded by a Kenya’s biggest jump-off party which happened on 31st December at Wild Waters in Mombasa.

    Within four months of its launch, NRG Radio became famous as a visual-radio FM station with online audio and video streaming. When it started its operation, this station scored one million hits on its website.

    NRG has implemented a new wave of radio technology, that includes modern production and control rooms. These rooms have visual integration, which enables people to stream live videos directly from the studio to any other content distribution network and other social media platforms.

    NRG radio frequency

    What frequency is NRG? NRG radio frequency in Nairobi is 97.1 FM. At this frequency, you can find popular musician presenter Timmy Tdat, Home Boys presenter known as Mwalimu Rachel, and so many other presenters. Previously, this radio used to broadcast only in Nairobi, but nowadays, people can tune in to this radio from different locations like Nairobi, Eldoret, Nakuru, Kisumu, and Mombasa.

    NRG radio gave birth to twin stations, which are Choice Radio and Fun X Radio. You can tune in Fun X on your radio, and it is available on 91.3 FM. You can also access Choice Radio at 87.7 FM. Choice Radio is an awesome station that contains sexy content, which makes it hard for the people to turn it off.

    NRG radio target audience

    So, which audience does the station target? Well, as we know, the main target of this radio is young people. NRG radio station targets those people who are aged 30 years and above. These people can understand the content and the advice given by this station since they are mature enough to understand each concept.

    On the other hand, Fun X targets young people who are aged between 13 years to 17 years old. Most often, this station seeks to cater to the needs of the teenagers, through the delivery of fun and adventurous content. NRG Radio strives to cater to all the needs of its audiences regardless of age.

    NRG Radio online

    Did you know that you can listen to NRG radio online? Well, the process is pretty simple. All you need to do is to open the NRG radio website. On this site, you have the option of either listening or watching live.
