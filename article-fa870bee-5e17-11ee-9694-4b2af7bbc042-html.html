    Frank E. Williams, 83 of Ellisville, MS passed away Wednesday, September 27, 2023, at South Central Regional Medical Center in Laurel, MS. He was born Monday, March 4, 1940 in Laurel, Mississippi.

    Visitation will be held on Sunday, October 1, 2023, from 2:00PM, until 4:30PM at West Ellisville Baptist Church in Ellisville, MS. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, October 1, 2023, at 4:30PM at West Ellisville Baptist Church in Ellisville, MS. Burial will follow in Ellisville City Cemetery in Ellisville, Mississippi. Bro. Jerry East will officiate. Ellisville Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements. 601-477-3797.

    Frank was a charter member of West Ellisville Baptist Church where he served as a Deacon. He was a Gideon, Alderman for the City of Ellisville for 32 years. He enjoyed fishing and restoring older vehicles.

    He was preceded in death by his first wife, Judy Gatewood Williams; father, Frank C. Williams; mother, Elna Sessions Williams; brother, Donald Williams; sisters, Sarah Colglazier and June McLemore; daughter-in-law, Adaâ€™Carol Williams.

    Survivors include his wife, Doris Kyle Williams; son, Jeffrey Williams; step-son, Anthony Roland; step-daughter, Melanie Dukes (Scott); grandchildren, Brooke Herrington (Aaron), Reed Roland and Ryan Roland; great grandchildren, Aubree Herrington and Abby Herrington; sister, Yvonne Hutto and a host of nieces and nephews.

    Pallbearers will be Deacons of West Ellisville Baptist Church. 

    In lieu of flowers donations may be made to West Ellisville Baptist Church Building Fund.

    An online guestbook may be signed at www.ellisvillefuneralhome.com
